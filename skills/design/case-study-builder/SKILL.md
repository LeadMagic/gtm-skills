# Case Study Builder

## Overview
A case study is the most versatile proof asset in B2B sales. It works in decks, one-pagers, emails, and discovery calls. This skill produces case studies in sales-ready format — concise enough for a 30-second read, detailed enough to close a deal.

## When to Use
- "Create a case study"
- "Write a customer success story"
- "Build proof content"
- "Document customer results"

## Step-by-Step Process
### Phase 1: Information Gathering
Collect: customer profile, challenge before your product, solution implemented, results achieved (with metrics), customer quote.

### Phase 2: Structure
1. Executive Summary (3 lines)
2. The Challenge (what was broken)
3. The Solution (how you helped)
4. The Results (metrics, quotes)
5. The Future (expansion, ongoing value)

### Phase 3: Sales-Ready Format
Produce two versions: 1-page summary for quick sharing, full version for late-stage deals.

## Output Format
Case study document with both 1-page and full versions, formatted for sales use with clear metrics and quotes.

## Common Pitfalls
1. **No metrics** — a case study without numbers is a testimonial.
2. **Too long** — reps cannot read a 5-page case study before a call.
3. **Generic results** — "improved efficiency" means nothing. "Reduced time-to-close by 40%" is proof.
