# One-Pager Builder

## Overview
A one-pager is exactly what it sounds like — one page that tells a prospect everything they need to know. Scannable in 30 seconds. Memorable enough to keep. Valuable enough that a champion forwards it internally.

## When to Use
- "Create a one-pager" / "Build a leave-behind" / "Make a product overview sheet"

## Step-by-Step Process
### Phase 1: Variant Selection
- Product Overview: Problem → Solution → 3 Differentiators → Proof → CTA
- Post-Meeting Leave-Behind: Reinforce discussion, keep momentum
- Champion Enablement: Arm internal champion to sell for you
- Trade Show Handout: Quick intro driving follow-up

### Phase 2: Content Structure
1. Problem statement (one sentence) | 2. Your solution | 3. 3 differentiators | 4. Proof point (one metric or quote) | 5. CTA with contact info

### Design Principles
One page literally. Scannable in 30 seconds. Bold headers, short bullets, whitespace. Logo, website, specific contact person.

## Output Format
One-pager copy with layout guidance (visual hierarchy, sections, CTA placement).

## Common Pitfalls
1. **Too much text** — if it takes 5 minutes to read, it is a brochure, not a one-pager.
2. **No clear CTA** — what should they do after reading?
3. **Generic differentiators** — "best-in-class" means nothing without specifics.
