# Objection Handling — Framework Notes

Reference tables for `SKILL.md`. Apply named frameworks to justify recommendations — do not cite as decoration.

## Primary frameworks

- **Force Management — Command of the Message** — Objections reveal VMF gaps
- **Brent Adamson — Challenger reframing** — Commercial teaching before answer. `references/brent-adamson-challenger.md`
- **Anthony Iannarino — Eat Their Lunch** — Competitive leveling vs status quo. `references/anthony-iannarino-sales-discipline.md`
- **Sandler — Pain Funnel** — Explore behind surface objection
- **Todd Caponi — Transparency Sale** — Lead with weaknesses where honest
- **Matthew Dixon — JOLT** — For indecision after fit, load `buyer-indecision`

## Authoritative foundations

Use **AER** (Acknowledge → Explore → Redirect) for live objections. Pre-handle
in outreach when objections are predictable (price, timing, competitor).
Separate **objection** (pushback) from **indecision** (FOMU) — different playbooks.

## Process phases

- **Phase 1 — Taxonomy:** Six categories (value, timing, competition, authority, status quo, trust)
- **Phase 2 — AER responses:** Per category + competitor battlecards
- **Phase 3 — Pre-handling:** Email/LI copy that addresses objections early
- **Phase 4 — Coaching:** Record, tag, monthly top-5 objection review
