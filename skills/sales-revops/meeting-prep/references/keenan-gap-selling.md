## Core Model

Gap Selling is a **Problem Centric™** methodology: buyers don't purchase products — they purchase movement from **Current State** to **Future State**. The seller's job is to diagnose the **Gap** (distance + cost of staying put) before prescribing a solution.

| Component | What to uncover | CRM mapping |
|---|---|---|
| **Current State** | Situation, problems, impact, root cause, emotions | SPICED Situation + Pain; MEDDICC Identify Pain + Metrics |
| **Future State** | Measurable outcomes buyer wants — not your demo | SPICED Impact + Decision criteria |
| **The Gap** | Quantified distance; urgency to change | SPICED Critical Event; MEDDICC Metrics |

**Rule:** ~25% of total sales cycle on discovery (Keenan). One discovery call is not enough for complex B2B.

---

## Current State — Five Elements

Uncover all five before presenting. Missing one = incomplete diagnosis.

| # | Element | Discovery questions |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Situation** | Walk me through how [process] works today — team, tools, volume? |
| 2 | **Problem** | What's not working? Technical vs business problems? |
| 3 | **Impact** | What does that cost in dollars, time, risk, or missed revenue? |
| 4 | **Root cause** | Why does this keep happening — not symptoms, underlying cause? |
| 5 | **Emotions** | How does this affect you / the team day to day? |

---

## Four-Step Discovery Process

Run in order — do not skip to solution:

1. **Discover facts** — Current state situation (environment, team, process)
2. **Discover problems** — What's broken; separate technical from business
3. **Discover impact** — Quantify cost of problems (buyer-stated, not vendor-assumed)
4. **Discover root cause** — Why problems persist; status quo drivers

Only after all four → map **Future State** → size the **Gap** → tie solution as bridge.

---

## Future State Talk Track

> "If we solved [root cause], what would [metric/process] look like in 6–12 months? What number would prove success?"

Future state must be **buyer-defined and measurable** — not a product tour.

---

## Problem Identification Chart (PIC)

Build **before** calls (team exercise):

| Problem your product solves | Business impact if unsolved | Root cause |
|---|---|---|
| … | … | … |

Reps listen for PIC matches, then drill into impact + root cause when they hear a hit.

---

## When to Use

- First and second discovery calls (primary framework)
- Deal reviews when pipeline is "happy" but not closing
- Qualification when SPICED Pain exists but Impact/CE are weak
- Pricing conversations — price scales with gap size, not feature list

## When NOT to Use

- Pure transactional / SKU sales with no discovery window
- As a substitute for MEDDICC on enterprise deals — **combine**: Gap Selling runs discovery; MEDDICC scores evidence
- Scripted demo-first calls — Gap Selling requires diagnosis before prescription

---

## Checklist — Pre-Proposal Gate

- [ ] All five current-state elements documented with buyer words
- [ ] Impact quantified (buyer stated a number or agreed to one)
- [ ] Root cause identified (not just symptoms)
- [ ] Future state agreed — buyer can describe success without your slides
- [ ] Gap explicitly acknowledged: "Status quo is unacceptable because ___"
- [ ] No **happy ears** — enthusiasm validated with evidence, not assumed

---

## Anti-Patterns (Happy Ears & More)

| Anti-pattern | Signal | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| **Happy ears** | Prospect nods; rep hears "yes" without impact/root cause | Require quantified impact before advancing stage |
| **Need-based selling** | "They need our product" | Reframe: sell the gap, not the need |
| **Premature prescription** | Demo before diagnosis complete | 25% cycle rule — stay in discovery |
| **Vendor-defined metrics** | "You'll save 30%" | Buyer must name KPI + baseline |
| **Feature pitching** | Slides before gap is visible | Future state conversation first |
| **Single-call discovery** | All five elements in 20 min | Schedule follow-up; depth over speed |

## SPICED + Gap Selling + MEDDICC

| Framework | Role in stack |
|---|---|
| **Gap Selling** | Diagnostic depth — current/future/gap, root cause, emotions |
| **SPICED** | CRM field structure — progressive capture across calls |
| **MEDDICC** | Evidence scorecard — go/no-go at stage gates |

Same deal, different lenses. Impact in Gap Selling → populates SPICED Impact + MEDDICC Metrics.
