# Content Syndication & Distribution

## Overview
Creating content is 20% of the work. Distributing it is 80%. Most teams publish
and pray. This skill builds a systematic distribution engine that turns one piece
of content into 15+ touchpoints across channels and extends reach through paid
syndication and partnerships.

## When to Use
- "Distribute our content"
- "Syndication strategy"
- "Amplify content reach"
- "Content distribution plan"

## Step-by-Step Process

### Phase 1: Content Atomization
One pillar piece → 15+ derivative assets:

**Pillar:** Research report, original data study, or definitive guide

**Derivatives:**
- Twitter/X thread (10-15 tweets, key insights)
- LinkedIn post (long-form, personal voice)
- LinkedIn carousel (5-7 slides, visual insights)
- Email newsletter (your angle + key takeaways)
- Short-form video (60-90s, one key insight)
- Podcast episode (deep dive, interview format)
- Guest post pitch (tailored angle for partner publication)
- SlideShare/Canva deck (visual summary)
- Infographic (one key data point)
- Quote cards (3-5, for social)
- Reddit post (in relevant subreddit, community-first)
- Newsletter sponsorship copy (3 variations)
- Sales enablement card (why prospects should read this)
- Webinar topic (expand on one section)
- LinkedIn ad creative (lead gen magnet)

### Phase 2: Organic Distribution Sequence
- **Day 0:** Publish on your blog/site
- **Day 0:** Twitter/X thread
- **Day 1:** LinkedIn post (personal founder/exec account)
- **Day 1:** Email to segmented list
- **Day 2:** Company LinkedIn page post
- **Day 3:** Reddit (where relevant, no link in top-level post)
- **Day 4:** LinkedIn carousel (repackaged top insights)
- **Day 7:** Short video (1 key insight)
- **Day 14:** Cross-link from related blog posts
- **Day 30:** Repromote — "In case you missed it"

### Phase 3: Paid Syndication
- **Newsletter sponsorships:** Sponsor 2-3 newsletters in your ICP's inbox.
  Test small ($500-1K) buys before scaling.
- **LinkedIn sponsored content:** Target by job title + company size + industry.
  Lead gen form attachment.
- **Content syndication networks:** NetLine, TechTarget, Industry Dive — pay
  per lead, high volume, lower quality. Use with strong lead scoring.
- **Retargeting:** Pixel your pillar content page, retarget visitors with
  demo/book-a-call ads for 30 days.

### Phase 4: Partner Amplification
- **Co-marketing:** Partners share your content to their audience (you reciprocate)
- **Customer advocacy:** Ask 3-5 customers to share with their networks
- **Employee advocacy:** Enable your team to share (provide pre-written posts)
- **Influencer/analyst:** Share with 5-10 industry voices who might amplify

### Phase 5: Measurement
Track per piece: views, downloads, leads generated, pipeline influenced,
revenue attributed. Report: cost per lead by channel, pipeline velocity
from syndication vs organic, highest-converting derivative format.

## Output Format
Syndication plan with: atomization map, 30-day distribution calendar,
paid syndication budget and targets, partner amplification list, and
measurement dashboard.
