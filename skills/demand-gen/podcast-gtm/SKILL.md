# Podcast GTM

## Overview
Podcast guesting is the highest-leverage GTM channel most founders ignore.
One 45-minute appearance → 10+ content derivatives, backlinks, social proof,
and direct pipeline from listeners who self-qualify by listening for 30+ minutes.
This skill covers booking strategy through pipeline conversion.

## Step-by-Step Process

### Phase 1: Target Show Selection
Prioritize podcasts by ICP overlap, not audience size:
- **Tier 1:** Shows your ICP listens to (even if audience is 500). The 500 right
  people > 50,000 random listeners.
- **Tier 2:** Industry shows with good production quality and backlinks
- **Tier 3:** General business/startup shows for brand reach

Research method: Ask 10 customers what podcasts they listen to. Search Apple
Podcasts/Spotify for industry keywords. Check competitor guest appearances.

### Phase 2: Booking Outreach
- **Research:** Listen to 2-3 recent episodes. Reference specific episodes in
  your pitch — proves you're not spraying.
- **Topic pitch:** 3 specific topics you can cover, each with a counterintuitive
  angle. Not "I'd love to come on your show" — "Your episode with [Guest] on
  [Topic] made me think about [Your Angle]. I'd love to discuss..."
- **Credentials:** Relevant data, unique experience, controversial take.
  "I've sent 1M+ cold emails and here's what I learned..."
- **Follow-up:** 2 touches max. Podcast hosts are busy.

### Phase 3: Interview Preparation
- **3 key messages:** What do you want listeners to remember? Repeat each 2-3 times
  in different ways.
- **Signature story:** One memorable customer story or personal experience.
- **Counterintuitive take:** What do you believe that most people in your industry
  don't? This is what gets shared.
- **CTA:** Where should listeners go? Simple, single URL. Free tool > newsletter >
  book a call (in that order of conversion).

### Phase 4: Content Repurposing
One episode → 10+ assets:
- Pull-quote social graphics (3-5)
- Twitter/X thread (highlights)
- LinkedIn post (personal reflection)
- Blog post (expanded version of your key argument)
- Newsletter mention
- YouTube Short / TikTok clip (best 60 seconds)
- "As seen on" media section for your website
- Backlink in guest profile (SEO value)

### Phase 5: Pipeline Conversion
- **Show notes link:** Specific landing page for that podcast's audience
- **Episode promo:** You promote the episode to your audience (hosts love this,
  they'll invite you back)
- **Listener outreach:** "Heard you on [Podcast]" is a great cold email opener
- **Track:** Unique URL in show notes, track signups and pipeline from podcast
  appearances. You'll likely find podcast leads convert at 2-3x your average rate
  because they've spent 45 minutes with you before booking a call.

## Common Pitfalls

1. **Webinar as product demo.** Prospects attend for insights, not a sales pitch. Fix: 80% educational content, 20% product mention. Lead with counterintuitive insights.
2. **One-and-done syndication.** Publishing once and moving on. Fix: one pillar piece → 15+ derivatives across channels over 30 days.
3. **Measuring paid social on clicks.** Clicks and CPL don't equal pipeline. Fix: track pipeline generated and cost per opportunity from each channel.
