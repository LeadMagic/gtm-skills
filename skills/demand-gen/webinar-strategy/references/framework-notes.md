## Primary frameworks

- **CEB Challenger — Commercial teaching** — Teach something counterintuitive; reframe the problem; lead to your methodology (not product demo).
- **TOPO Webinar Framework** — Topic validation, promotion architecture, production standards, post-event pipeline.
- **Chris Walker — Demand creation** — 80% education / 20% product; measure influenced pipeline, not registration volume alone; 90-day program eval.

## Topic validation (Challenger tests)

| Test | Pass criteria |
|---|---|
| Challenges ICP assumption? | "Why your [belief] is wrong" angle |
| Forwardable to boss? | Executive-relevant insight |
| Natural problem → solution path? | Leads to conversation your product solves |

**Fail:** Product demo disguised as webinar → Walker pitfall #1.

## Speaker sourcing

| Type | When to use | Credibility |
|---|---|---|
| Internal expert | Default — best thinker | Category authority |
| Customer speaker | When possible | 10× case study credibility |
| Analyst / partner | Shared audience | Third-party validation |
| 5-person panel | Avoid | Lowest engagement |

## Promotion architecture (21-day build)

| Day | Channel | Asset |
|---|---|---|
| D-21 | Organic social | Registration page live |
| D-14 | Email 1 | Segmented house list |
| D-10 | Speaker social | LinkedIn event |
| D-7 | Email 2 | "What you'll learn" angle |
| D-5 | Paid + partners | Lookalike + co-promo |
| D-3 | Email 3 + DMs | Last chance + VIP targets |
| D-1 | Reminder | Calendar link |

Pair with Walker **frequency** — webinar clips feed LinkedIn/newsletter for 30 days post-event.

## Production standards

- 45 min max (30 content + 15 Q&A)
- Minimal-text slides; one idea per slide
- Moderator separate from presenter
- Recorded backup for live demo segments

## Post-webinar pipeline

| Timing | Action | Segment |
|---|---|---|
| Hour 0 | Recording + slides | All registrants |
| Day 1 | Written Q&A answers | Attendees |
| Day 3–5 | SDR blitz | Hot (full time + questions) |
| Day 7 | "What you missed" | No-shows |
| Day 14+ | Repurpose | Blog, clips, podcast → Pattern 26 stack |

## Measurement — pipeline, not registrations

| Metric | Walker-aligned definition |
|---|---|
| Attended vs registered | Engagement quality |
| Hot attendee → meeting rate | SDR conversion |
| Influenced pipeline $ | CRM + self-reported |
| Content derivatives published | Frequency / dark social |
| 90-day program ROI | Cohort revenue, not last-click |

## Process phases

1. **Topic selection** — Challenger validation + ICP forward test
2. **Speaker sourcing** — Internal / customer / partner brief
3. **Promotion (21-day)** — Email, social, paid, partner calendar
4. **Production** — Run-of-show, slides, moderator, backup recording
5. **Post-event pipeline** — Segmentation, SDR blitz, repurposing map
