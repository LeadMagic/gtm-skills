# Paid Social Strategy (B2B)

## Overview
Paid social for B2B is fundamentally different from B2C — you're not optimizing
for clicks, you're optimizing for pipeline. LinkedIn is the primary platform,
but Meta retargeting and emerging channels (TikTok for B2B, Reddit ads) are
increasingly viable. This skill covers platform strategy, audience architecture,
creative testing, and pipeline measurement.

## When to Use
- "Launch paid social"
- "LinkedIn ads strategy"
- "B2B advertising plan"
- "Paid media strategy"

## Step-by-Step Process

### Phase 1: Platform Selection
- **LinkedIn:** Primary. Job title + company + industry targeting. Lead gen forms.
  Thought leadership ads (document ads). $5-15 CPL typical. Best for $10K+ ACV.
- **Meta (Facebook/Instagram):** Retargeting only for most B2B. Website visitors,
  content engagers, video viewers. Lower CPMs, broader audiences.
- **TikTok:** Emerging for B2B. Organic-style creative. Reaching younger
  decision-makers. Test with $500 budget before scaling.
- **Reddit:** Community-first. Sponsored posts in relevant subreddits.
  Authentic voice required.
- **YouTube:** In-stream ads for product demos, customer stories, thought leadership.

### Phase 2: Audience Architecture
- **Cold audience:** ICP targeting (title, industry, company size). Content/thought
  leadership ads. Goal: content engagement, not demo request.
- **Warm audience:** Website visitors (pixel), content engagers, video viewers (50%+).
  Case study/social proof ads. Goal: demo/book-a-call.
- **Hot audience:** Pricing page visitors, demo requesters (didn't convert).
  Direct offer ads. Goal: convert.
- **Account-based:** Upload target account list, target employees at those companies.
  Coordinated ad + outbound motion.

### Phase 3: Creative Strategy
- **Thought leadership:** "The 3 attribution mistakes costing you 40% of pipeline"
  → lead gen form for full guide. Document ad format on LinkedIn.
- **Social proof:** Customer quote + results + logo. Video testimonial > text.
- **Product demo:** 30-60 second product walkthrough, specific use case.
- **Event/webinar:** "Live: How [Company] scaled outbound to $10M pipeline" → registration.

### Phase 4: Budget & Bidding
- Start: $3-5K/month test budget per platform
- Bid strategy: Max delivery for awareness, manual CPC for lead gen
- 80/20 rule: 80% budget on proven campaigns, 20% on tests
- Kill campaigns with CPL > 2x target after 500+ impressions
- Retargeting budget: 15-25% of total

### Phase 5: Pipeline Measurement
Don't measure ads on clicks and CPL. Measure:
- Pipeline generated (opportunities from ad-influenced accounts)
- Pipeline influenced (ads touched account before opportunity created)
- Cost per opportunity (total ad spend / opportunities)
- Cost per closed-won (total ad spend / closed-won deals)
- Ad-influenced pipeline as % of total pipeline

## Output Format
Paid social plan with: platform allocation, audience architecture, creative
briefs, budget model, and pipeline measurement framework.
