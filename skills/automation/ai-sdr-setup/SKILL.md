# AI SDR Setup

## Overview
AI SDRs handle prospecting research, email drafting, and sequence management at a speed no human team can match. But deployment without structure creates chaos — generic messages, no handoff logic, and brand risk.

## When to Use
- "Set up an AI SDR"
- "Automate our outbound prospecting"
- "Deploy 11x / Artisan / AiSDR / Jason AI"

## Step-by-Step Process
### Week 1: Foundation
- Define ICP and qualification criteria
- Build signal-to-action routing rules
- Configure brand voice and compliance guardrails

### Week 2: Pilot
- Run on 200 contacts maximum
- Human reviews every message before send
- Track reply rates, bounce rates, positive reply rate

### Week 3: Tune
- A/B test message variants
- Adjust personalization depth
- Tune signal thresholds

### Week 4: Scale and Handoff
- Increase volume to 500/week
- Configure human handoff triggers (positive reply, specific objection, competitor mention, pricing question, technical question)
- Set up Slack notifications for handoffs

## Output Format
AI SDR deployment plan with 4-week timeline, signal routing rules, handoff triggers, compliance checklist, and performance baseline metrics.

## Common Pitfalls
1. **No human review in week 1-2.** AI generates plausible-sounding but wrong content.
2. **Sending at full volume immediately.** Start small, validate, then scale.
3. **No handoff triggers.** AI keeps replying when a human should take over.
4. **Brand voice undefined.** AI defaults to generic corporate tone.
5. **No compliance baked in.** AI must check suppression lists, CAN-SPAM, and GDPR.
