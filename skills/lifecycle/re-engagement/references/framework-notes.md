# Re-Engagement — Framework Notes

Reference tables for `SKILL.md`. Lead with what's changed — not "we miss you."

---

## Primary frameworks

- **Win-Back Campaign Framework** — Segment by dormancy depth
- **Retention Science Methodology** — Reactivation cost vs acquisition
- **Reforge Lifecycle Marketing** — Sunset and list hygiene policies

---

## Segmentation matrix

| Segment | Dormancy | Sequence | Offer |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cold lead | 3–6 mo | 3-touch / 14d | New resource angle |
| Frozen lead | 6–12 mo | Condensed | "We've changed" |
| Dead lead | 12+ mo | 1 email | Suppress if no reply |
| Lapsed user (paying) | Usage −50% / 30d | Health play | Not win-back |
| Recent churn | <90d | Win-back 3-touch / 21d | 1 mo free, feature unlock |
| Old churn | 90d+ | Annual check-in only | Low probability |

---

## "What's new" inventory (required before outreach)

Document since last engagement:
1. Top 3 product improvements
2. 2 new integrations/features
3. 1–2 industry case studies
4. Pricing/packaging changes that benefit them
5. New research/benchmarks

---

## Sunset policy

| Population | After failed sequence | Final action |
|---|---|---|
| Dormant leads | 3-touch no reply | 6mo cooldown → 1 attempt → suppress |
| Churned customers | Win-back no reply | Annual check-in only |
| GDPR | 24mo no engagement | Delete/anonymize |
