## Authoritative foundations

Onboarding succeeds when the customer hits a **defined activation event** tied
to their Desired Outcome — not when they complete a checklist of tasks.
Document sales handoff: impact metrics, stakeholders, success criteria.

## Process phases

- **Phase 1 — Handoff:** SPICED/Desired Outcome doc from sales → CS
- **Phase 2 — Kickoff:** Align milestones, owners, TTV target date
- **Phase 3 — Activation:** Guide to first value (human + in-app)
- **Phase 4 — Habit:** Usage monitoring → health score → expansion trigger
