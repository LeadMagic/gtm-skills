## Authoritative foundations

Email finding is the **last mile** of list building — optimize for **validity rate**
(≥98%) not coverage alone. Bounce damage hurts domain reputation across the
entire outbound program.

## Process phases

- **Phase 1 — Input quality:** Verified LinkedIn URL or name+domain required
- **Phase 2 — Waterfall:** Cheap providers first → premium fallback
- **Phase 3 — Verification:** Never skip verify step before sequencer
- **Phase 4 — QA sample:** Manual spot-check 5% of new lists
