# Gifting Platforms Comparison

**Last updated:** June 2025

| Platform | Strength | CRM | Best tier | Typical cost |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Sendoso** | Revenue team UX, eGift + physical | SF, HS, strong | 2–3 + approved Tier 1 | Platform + per-send |
| **Alyce** | Recipient picks gift; compliance | Enterprise MAP/CRM | Enterprise 1-to-1 | Higher ACV deals |
| **Reachdesk** | Global warehouse, ABM bundles | SF, HS | 1-to-few global | Per-touch |
| **Postal.io** | Handwritten + direct mail scale | Integrations | 1-to-few mailers | Per-piece |
| **Manual / Ruhlin-style** | Maximum personalization | CRM log manual | Tier 1 only | Time + concierge |

## Selection Matrix

| Need | Pick |
|---|---|
| SDR self-serve eGift with caps | Sendoso |
| "Let them choose" (reduce wrong gift) | Alyce |
| International ABM | Reachdesk |
| Book/report mailer at scale | Postal.io |
| CRO bespoke gift | Manual + Giftology |

## Stack Integration

```
Clay/CRM research → gift-brief.md → Platform send → CRM log → SDR call 24h
```

Load `gtm-tool-cost-model` for platform TCO.

## Not gifting platforms

- **Swag stores (Custom Ink, etc.)** — logo apparel; not Giftology
- **Amazon wishlists** — policy/compliance risk; avoid enterprise
