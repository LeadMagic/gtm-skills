# Gifting Budget by ABM Tier

Align with `abm-strategy` ITSMA tiers.

## Annual Budget per Account

| Tier | Accounts | $/account/year | Sends/year | Personalization |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| 1 — 1-to-1 | 5–15 | $500–2,000 | 2–4 | Giftology custom |
| 2 — 1-to-few | 15–50 | $150–500 | 1–2 | Cluster + personal note |
| 3 — 1-to-many | 50–200+ | $25–75 | 1 | eGift / useful item |

## Team Budget Example ($5M ARR ABM program)

| Tier | Count | $/acct | Total |

## Role Permissions

| Role | Tier 1 send | Tier 2 | Tier 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| AE | Propose + manager approve | Self-serve cap $200 | eGift only |
| SDR | No Tier 1 alone | eGift cap $75 | eGift |
| Marketing | Fund programs | Mailer campaigns | Automated |
| Founder | Tier 1 concierge | — | — |

## ROI Guardrails

Pause program if:
- Cost per meeting > $500 without enterprise ACV justification
- Zero meetings from last 50 Tier 3 eGifts
- Policy violation incident
