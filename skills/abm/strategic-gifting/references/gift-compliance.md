# Gift Compliance — Buyer Policies

**Not legal advice.** Engage counsel for regulated industries.

## Pre-Send Checklist

- [ ] Asked champion: "What is your company's gift acceptance policy?"
- [ ] Documented limit ($ or item type) in CRM
- [ ] Industry check: government, healthcare, banking often **$0**
- [ ] Gift value under limit (include shipping/tax)
- [ ] Not during active RFP negotiation / legal redlines
- [ ] Recorded in vendor disclosure if required

## Common Limits (indicative)

| Sector | Typical cap |
|---|---|
| Tech (commercial) | $75–250 per gift |
| Financial services | $25–100 or prohibited |
| Government / public sector | Often prohibited |
| Healthcare | Strict — often no gifts to prescribers |

## Anti-Bribery Test

Gift must pass:
1. **Transparency** — disclosed if asked
2. **Proportionality** — reasonable for relationship marketing
3. **No quid pro quo** — not conditioned on award of business
4. **Documentation** — CRM story + business purpose

## Sendoso / Platform Controls

- Per-user spend caps
- Manager approval > threshold
- Block list domains (`.gov`, etc.)
- Audit export for finance

## When Legal Says No

Use non-gift value: executive briefing, custom research, intro to customer peer —
`abm-1-to-1` content plays.
