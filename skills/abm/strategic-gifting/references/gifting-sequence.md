# Gifting Sequence Timeline

Coordinate with `abm-1-to-few` (mail arrives before email reference).

## Enterprise B2B SaaS (Typical)

| Stage | Day | Gift? | Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-outreach | — | No | — |
| First meeting booked | — | No | — |
| Post-discovery | D3–5 | Optional small | Gift 1 personal |
| Pilot / POC start | W2 | Team snack eGift | Low risk |
| Mid-eval | W3–4 | Team | Gift 3 (catered, useful) |
| Proposal / legal | — | **No** | — |
| Verbal yes | — | No | — |
| Closed Won | D1–3 | Thank you | Gift 2 family optional |
| 90d post-close | — | Check-in | Small retention |

## Stalled deal

**Do not send gift to unstick.** Use `buyer-indecision` (JOLT).

## Event-triggered

| Event | Gift |
|---|---|
| Spoke at conference | Book related to talk topic |
| Podcast guest | Reference episode in note |
| No-show to meeting | No gift — reschedule |

## Email coordination

**Week 2 pattern (1-to-few):**
- Day 1: Mailer ships
- Day 3–5: Mail arrives
- Day 5: Email Touch 1 references mailer (not gift brag)
