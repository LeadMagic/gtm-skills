# Support SLA Matrix

| Priority | Definition | First response | Resolution target | Escalation |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|
| **P1** | Production down / data loss | 15 min | 4 hr | VP Eng + CS lead |
| **P2** | Major feature broken | 1 hr | 8 hr | L2 same day |
| **P3** | Degraded / workaround exists | 4 hr | 2 business days | L2 if missed |
| **P4** | How-to / billing question | 8 hr | 3 business days | — |

**CSAT:** Survey on close; score ≤2 auto-escalates to manager.

Platform config: Zendesk SLA policies / Intercom team inboxes.
