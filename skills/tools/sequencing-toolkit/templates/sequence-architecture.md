# Standard Sequence Architecture

**Motion:** Outbound SDR · **Length:** 14 days · **Channels:** Email + LinkedIn + call

| Step | Day | Channel | Objective | Words |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | Email | Problem hook (PVP) | 50–75 |
| 2 | 2 | LinkedIn | Connect / soft touch | — |
| 3 | 3 | Email | Value / insight | 75–100 |
| 4 | 5 | Call | Voicemail | 30 sec |
| 5 | 7 | Email | Social proof | 50–75 |
| 6 | 12 | Email | Break-up | 40–60 |

Copy frameworks: `cold-email-copywriting` → email-frameworks.md  
A/B: subject + opener; winner = positive reply rate.
