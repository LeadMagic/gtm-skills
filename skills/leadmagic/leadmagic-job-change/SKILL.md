# LeadMagic Job Change Detection

## Overview
Job changes are the highest-signal trigger in B2B sales. A champion moving to a new company creates an immediate warm introduction. A decision-maker departing mid-deal is a risk signal. This skill covers job change monitoring: detecting moves, routing alerts, and triggering the right outreach.

## When to Use
- "Track job changes for my contacts"
- "Alert me when a champion changes jobs"
- "Monitor career moves in my pipeline"
- "Detect job changes for trigger-based outreach"

## Step-by-Step Process
### Phase 1: Contact Monitoring
Submit contacts to job change detection. The system monitors career moves and alerts when a change is detected.

### Phase 2: Alert Routing
- Champion departure → alert rep immediately → outreach to new company within 24 hours
- Decision-maker departure → flag deal risk → manager review
- Prospect job change → new outreach opportunity at new company

### Phase 3: Outreach Triggers
When a contact changes jobs:
1. Congratulate on the new role
2. Reference existing relationship context
3. Offer to continue the conversation at new company
4. Ask for introduction to their replacement (if champion)

## Output Format
Job change alert with contact details, old role, new role, new company, and recommended action.

## Common Pitfalls
1. **No champion tracking** — champion departure is the #1 churn predictor and missed expansion opportunity.
2. **Generic outreach** — "congrats on the new role" without context wastes the warm relationship.
3. **Slow response** — job change outreach must happen within days, not weeks. The window closes fast.
