## Authoritative foundations

LinkedIn's Social Selling Index (SSI) measures four dimensions: professional
brand, finding the right people, engaging with insights, and building relationships.
SSI leaders create more opportunities and hit quota at higher rates — use as
diagnostic, not vanity score.

## Process phases

- **Phase 1 — Profile + ICP map:** Optimize profile; build target account/contact list
- **Phase 2 — Engage with insight:** Comment, share POV, avoid pitch-slapping
- **Phase 3 — Warm outreach:** Reference prior engagement; Seidl relationship map for enterprise
- **Phase 4 — Convert:** Meeting ask after value exchange; route to `meeting-prep`
