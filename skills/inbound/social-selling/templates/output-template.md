# Social Selling — Deliverable

## Deliverable spec

Social selling playbook with profile optimization checklist, DM sequences
(3-5 touches), Sales Navigator saved-search templates, content calendar
(2-3 posts/week themes), and SSI tracking dashboard.

## Quality check

- [ ] Profile optimized (headline, about, featured, activity)
- [ ] DM sequences written and tested (not pitch-first)
- [ ] Sales Navigator search templates configured
- [ ] SSI baseline score recorded
- [ ] Content calendar established
