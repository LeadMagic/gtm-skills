## Authoritative foundations

High-converting B2B landing pages lead with **outcome + proof + single CTA** —
not feature lists. Match page variant to traffic source (paid, organic, outbound)
and measure pipeline contribution, not clicks alone.

## Process phases

- **Phase 1 — Intent match:** Source/keyword → persona → primary outcome
- **Phase 2 — Structure:** Hero, proof, objection handling, CTA, thank-you/next step
- **Phase 3 — Build + test:** Mobile, speed, form length, social proof placement
- **Phase 4 — Measure:** Form-to-SQL rate, not vanity traffic
