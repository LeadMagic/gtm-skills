## Primary Frameworks

### 1. Diagnostic Selling (Discovery)

Three-step discovery model for "DISCO" calls:

| Step | Goal | Talk track |
|---|---|---|
| **Prospect's self-diagnosis** | What they think is wrong | "What do you believe is causing [pain] today?" |
| **Expert diagnosis** | Your hypothesis from research | "Teams like yours often see X driven by Y — does that match?" |
| **Unknown problems** | Problems they haven't named | "Have you looked at [second-order effect]?" |

Holland maps **12 types of sales problems** — use to classify what you uncover (GTMnow webinar).

### 2. Stellar Cold Email System

Five questions every email must answer:

1. **Did they get it?** — Deliverability (infra, not copy)
2. **Did they open it?** — Pattern-interrupt subject lines
3. **Did they read it?** — Length, scanability
4. **Are you relevant?** — Tie to metrics, problems, triggers, or unknowns
5. **Did you take care of them psychologically?** — No manipulation; buyer-first tone

**Seven Pillars of great cold email:** relevance to metrics · relevance to problems · trigger relevance · unknown-problem insight · psychological safety · structure · sequence design.

**Seven Deadly Sins:** generic spray · feature pitch · fake personalization · long walls of text · weak CTA · no sequence logic · activity KPIs over quality.

**Structure:** Hook → relevance (metric/problem/trigger) → value hypothesis → low-friction CTA.

### 3. Lead Weighting & SDR KPI Design

From Holland's SDR leadership work — replace flat meeting quotas:

| Motion | Weighting principle |
|---|---|
| **Inbound SDR** | ~50% speed-to-lead; remainder on qualified-meeting quality |
| **Outbound SDR** | Weight by persona/seniority — not all meetings equal |
| **All-bound** | Separate scorecards per motion — don't blend inbound + outbound KPIs |

**Anti-BANT-as-SDR-gate:** BANT on first touch kills conversion; AE validates qualification post-handoff with weighted credit.

## When to Use

- SDR team design, KPI fixes, inbound/outbound conflict
- Cold email + sequence audits (structure, not just copy tweaks)
- Discovery call prep when SPICED feels too shallow — add diagnostic layers
- Sales leadership diagnosing "too much pipeline, poor conversion"

## When NOT to Use

- Enterprise deal inspection — pair with MEDDICC, not replace
- Infrastructure/deliverability — use `email-deliverability`, Eric Nowoslawski (`eric-nowoslawski-outbound.md`)
- AI-native list building — pair with Jordan Crawford FIND/PQS for research layer

---

## Sequence Structure Checklist

- [ ] Touch 1 answers: relevant metric OR problem OR trigger
- [ ] No feature dump in first email
- [ ] Follow-ups add **new value angles**, not "just bumping"
- [ ] Meetings weighted by persona tier in comp plan
- [ ] Inbound and outbound reps have **separate** KPI systems if both exist

---

## Anti-Patterns

| Mistake | Why it fails | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Round-robin inbound, flat meeting quota | Reps book easiest contact, not best fit | Lead weighting by title/ACV |
| BANT on SDR first call | Kills early-stage conversion | Diagnostic discovery → AE validation |
| Activity-only KPIs | Volume without pipeline quality | Correlate KPIs to closed-won, not just meetings |
| Marketing vs Sales MQL war | Misdefined funnel stages | Shared definitions + speed-to-lead SLAs |
| Same sequence for all personas | Low relevance | Play-based messaging per segment |
