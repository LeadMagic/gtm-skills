# Cold Email Frameworks

Structural patterns for outbound copy. Pair with `sequence-touch-library.md` and `subject-line-patterns.md`.

---

## Framework comparison

| Framework | Structure | Best for | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| **PVP** (Jason Bay) | Pain → Value → Proof | SDR first touch | Too long if proof weak |
| **PAS** | Problem → Agitate → Solve | Clear acute pain | Agitate can feel harsh |
| **AIDA** | Attention → Interest → Desire → Action | Marketing-style | Low reply on cold |
| **BAB** | Before → After → Bridge | Transformation stories | Needs credible bridge |
| **QVC** | Question → Value → CTA | Peer tone | Question must be real |

---

## PVP (primary for B2B SaaS)

```
Pain: [Specific pain in their words — 1 sentence]
Value: [Outcome metric — not feature]
Proof: [Logo, stat, or micro-case — 1 line]
CTA: [Low-friction ask — question or 15-min call]
```

**Example skeleton:**

> [Pain: RevOps teams at [segment] often spend 10+ hrs/week fixing CRM data.]
> [Value: We cut enrichment + routing to <30 min for teams like [Logo].]
> [Proof: [Customer] went from X to Y in Z weeks.]
> Worth a quick look — open to [day] or [day]?

---

## PAS

1. **Problem:** Name the status quo failure
2. **Agitate:** Cost of inaction (quantify if possible)
3. **Solve:** Your approach in one sentence + CTA

Use when pain is **already felt** (trigger event, job post, tech stack signal).

---

## Touch sequence architecture

| Touch | Goal | Framework |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Pattern interrupt + relevance | PVP |
| 2 | New angle (social proof) | BAB |
| 3 | Breakup or value-add | Short question |

Max 3–4 touches per channel before call/LinkedIn (`cold-email-strategy`).

---

## Subject line rules

- 3–6 words; lowercase often tests better
- No RE:/FWD: deception
- Personalization: company name or trigger — not `{FIRST_NAME}` only
- See `subject-line-patterns.md`

---

## Quality checklist

- [ ] <120 words touch 1
- [ ] One CTA only
- [ ] No spam words (free, guarantee, act now)
- [ ] Reads on mobile without scroll
- [ ] Proof is verifiable
- [ ] Opt-out path where required

---

## Personalization tiers

| Tier | Data | Effort |
|---|---|---|
| T1 | Trigger + role + company | 5–10 min/account |
| T2 | Segment + persona | Template + merge |
| T3 | Industry only | Low — test reply rate |

Match tier to ACV (`abm-1-to-1` for T1).
