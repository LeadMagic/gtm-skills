## Authoritative foundations

Multi-channel means **one coordinated narrative** across email, phone, and
LinkedIn — not three independent spam streams. Channel order follows signal:
Phone Intent P1 → phone-first; low phone intent → email/LI lead.

## Process phases

- **Phase 1 — Cadence design:** Touch count, channel mix, spacing by segment
- **Phase 2 — Message parity:** Same core insight; format per channel
- **Phase 3 — CRM wiring:** Buckets/stages; disposition → next channel
- **Phase 4 — Optimize:** Reply/meeting rate by channel pair; cut losers
