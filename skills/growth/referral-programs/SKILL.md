# Referral Programs

## Overview
Referred customers convert at 2-3x the rate of cold-acquired leads and have 37% higher retention. A structured referral program turns happy customers into a predictable acquisition channel.

## When to Use
- "Build a referral program"
- "Set up partner referrals"
- "Create an affiliate program"
- "Incentivize customer referrals"

## Step-by-Step Process
### Phase 1: Program Structure
Define: who can refer (customers, partners, affiliates), what they earn (cash, credits, discounts), when they get paid (on signup, on conversion, on retention milestone).

### Phase 2: Tier Design
Tier 1 (entry): 10-15% commission, first-year revenue. Tier 2 (silver): 15-20%, 10+ referrals/year. Tier 3 (gold): 20-25%, 50+ referrals/year, co-marketing.

### Phase 3: Platform Selection
PartnerStack, Impact, or FirstPromoter for automated tracking, payment, and compliance.

## Output Format
Referral program design with tier structure, commission model, platform recommendation, and launch plan.

## Common Pitfalls
1. **Complex enrollment** — make referring as easy as sharing a link.
2. **Delayed payments** — pay within 30 days or partners lose trust.
3. **No referral prompts** — happy customers do not think to refer without being asked.
