# Referral Programs

## Overview

Word of mouth is the lowest-CAC, highest-conversion acquisition channel. A
referral from a trusted peer converts at 4-10x the rate of cold outreach. The
mistake: having no formal referral program and hoping customers spontaneously
refer you. This skill covers designing referral programs that customers
actually use — with the right incentives, frictionless mechanics, and tracking.

## Step-by-Step Process

### Phase 1: Program Design

**Referral types:** Customer referral (B2B), affiliate/partner referral,
employee referral, investor referral.

**Incentive models:**
| Model | Example | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Double-sided cash | "$100 to you, $100 to them" | High-ACV products |
| Product value | "1 month free for each referral" | SaaS with low marginal cost |
| Feature unlock | "Unlock premium features with 3 referrals" | Freemium, consumer |
| Status/priority | "Jump the waitlist" | Pre-launch, invite-only |
| Charitable donation | "$50 donated in your name" | Mission-driven brands |
| Swag/gifts | "Limited edition hoodie" | Community-driven |

### Phase 2: Economics

**Standard referral comp:** 10-15% of first-year contract value. One-time
payment on first payment. Pay on collection, not on sign-up (prevents fraud).

### Phase 3: Mechanics

**Referral flow:** Referrer gets unique link → shares with peers → peer signs
up (tagged with referrer) → peer becomes paying → referrer paid.

**Tier progression:** Base: 5% for 1-5 referrals. Silver: 10% for 6-15.
Gold: 15% for 16+. Add non-monetary benefits at each tier.

### Phase 4: Tools

Referral tracking: Referral Rock, PartnerStack, Rewardful, FirstPromoter,
Friendbuy. For B2B SaaS: PartnerStack or Rewardful.

## Implementation Checklist

- [ ] Referral link generated per customer (trackable)
- [ ] Incentive structure documented (3 tiers minimum)
- [ ] Fraud prevention: pay on collection, not sign-up
- [ ] Referral dashboard for referrers (track their impact)
- [ ] Monthly referral leaderboard for gamification

## Common Pitfalls

1. **Cash-only incentives.** "$50 per referral" underperforms "$50 for you,
   $50 for your friend" (social obligation). Fix: Double-sided rewards.
2. **Paying on sign-up, not collection.** 100 sign-ups, 0 paying = paid
   $5,000 for nothing. Fix: Pay when referral converts to paying.
3. **No referral dashboard.** Referrers don't know if their referrals
   converted. They stop sharing. Fix: Live dashboard for every referrer.
4. **Too complex.** 5-step referral funnel with verification. Fix: One
   click to generate link. One click to share. Automated tracking.
