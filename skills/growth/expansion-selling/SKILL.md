# Expansion Selling

## Overview
The most profitable revenue comes from existing customers. Expansion (upsells, cross-sells, seat growth) costs 5-10x less to acquire than new logos and compounds NRR. This skill builds systematic expansion motions triggered by product usage and customer behavior.

## When to Use
- "Build an expansion motion"
- "Increase NRR"
- "Design upsell triggers"
- "Create a land-and-expand strategy"

## Step-by-Step Process
### Phase 1: Expansion Triggers
- Usage approaching plan limits (seat count, API calls, storage)
- Feature adoption signals readiness for upgrade
- New department/team requesting access
- Organizational growth (hiring surge, new office)
- Competitor displacement opportunity

### Phase 2: Account Planning
For top accounts: map organizational structure, identify expansion opportunities, build stakeholder relationships across departments, document success metrics for internal selling.

### Phase 3: Expansion Playbooks
Per trigger: automated notification to CSM/rep, personalized outreach template, proof points from similar expansions, ROI case for the upgrade.

## Output Format
Expansion playbook with trigger catalog, account planning template, outreach sequences, and NRR tracking model.

## Common Pitfalls
1. **Expansion as afterthought** — integration into daily workflows matters more than quarterly reviews.
2. **One-size upsell** — different triggers need different approaches.
3. **No proof for expansion** — customer needs to see what others gained from upgrading.
