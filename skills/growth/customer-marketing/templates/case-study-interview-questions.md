# Case Study Interview Questions

**Customer:** ___ **Interviewee:** ___ **Role:** ___ **Interviewer:** ___

---

## Before (pain)

1. What was happening before you used [product]? Describe a typical week.
2. What was the measurable cost — time, money, risk, missed revenue?
3. What else did you try? Why didn't it work?

## Decision

4. Why did you choose us over alternatives?
5. Who else was involved in the decision? What mattered to each stakeholder?

## After (results)

6. What changed in the first 90 days? Be specific — hours saved, % improvement, revenue impact.
7. What surprised you — positive or negative?
8. What metrics do you track now that you didn't before?

## Advocacy

9. What would you tell a peer considering [product]?
10. Would you be open to: ☐ Named quote ☐ Logo ☐ G2 review ☐ Reference calls ☐ Webinar
