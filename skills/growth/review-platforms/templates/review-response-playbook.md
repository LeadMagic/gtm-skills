# Review Response Templates

## Positive (4–5 stars)

> Thank you, [Name] — we're glad [specific outcome they mentioned] has been valuable for [Company]. Our team loved partnering on [implementation/ use case]. If anything comes up, reach us at [support]. — [Name], [Title]

## Mixed (3 stars)

> Thanks for the honest feedback, [Name]. We're sorry [issue] fell short. We've [fixed / scheduled / assigned] [action]. I'd welcome a quick call to make this right — [email]. — [Name], [Title]

## Negative (1–2 stars)

> [Name], thank you for sharing this — I'm sorry your experience didn't match what we aim for. I'd like to understand more and help. I've asked [CS leader] to reach you directly at [contact]. We're also reviewing [process] internally. — [Name], [Title]

## Factual correction

> Thank you for your review. We want to clarify: [fact]. We'd welcome updating your experience — please contact [email]. — [Name], [Title]

**Rules:** No PHI, no contract terms, no arguing, <48h SLA.
