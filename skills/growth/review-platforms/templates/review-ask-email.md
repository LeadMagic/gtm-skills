# Review Ask Email Templates

Personal send from CSM or AE — not marketing blast. Include **direct review link** from vendor portal.

---

## Template A: Post go-live (30 days)

**Subject:** Quick favor — 5 min to share your experience?

Hi [First name],

It's been about a month since [Company] went live on [Product]. I hope [specific outcome — e.g., "the enrichment workflow"] is saving your team time.

If you have 5 minutes, would you share a brief review on [G2 / TrustRadius]? It helps teams like yours find us when they're evaluating solutions.

**Direct link:** [vendor portal review URL]

Happy to jump on a quick call if easier — I can capture your words and you approve before submit.

Thanks,
[Name], [Title]

---

## Template B: NPS promoter (9–10)

No pressure either way — I'll keep being your point of contact.

Best,
[Name]

---

## Rules

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| One customer = one platform ask per quarter max | Incentivize cash or gifts for reviews (ToS violation) |
| State time estimate honestly | Mass BCC from marketing@ |
| Offer to help draft from their words | Ghost-write fictional outcomes |
| Follow up once after 7 days | Pressure after "no" |

Track asks in CRM: field `review_ask_date`, `review_platform`, `review_status`.
