# Review Platform Comparison

*Last updated: June 2025 — verify pricing and features before vendor contracts.*

---

## Platform matrix

| Platform | Primary buyer | Review style | Strength | Weakness | Best ARR stage |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **G2** | Marketing + ops | Short–medium; high volume | Grid badges, intent data, broad SMB/MM | Shallow enterprise depth | $1M+ |
| **Capterra** | SMB comparison shoppers | Short | Paid search adjacency | Less enterprise credibility | <$5M |
| **TrustRadius** | Technical evaluators | Long, verified | Depth, TruVoice trust | Slower review velocity | $5M+ enterprise |
| **Gartner Peer Insights** | Procurement + IT | Structured enterprise | Tied to MQ research | Gated; longer cycle | $10M+ |
| **Product Hunt** | Early adopters | Launch comments | Spike awareness | Not sustained advocacy | Pre-PMF / launch |
| **SourceForge** | Legacy dev tools | Mixed | Niche traffic | Low B2B SaaS priority | Avoid unless vertical fit |

## Operational comparison

| Factor | G2 | TrustRadius | Gartner PI |
|---|---|---|---|
| Review time (reviewer) | ~5 min | 15–30 min | 20+ min |
| Recency weight | High | Medium | Medium |
| Intent data product | Yes (paid) | Limited | Limited |
| Badge marketing | Strong | Moderate | Enterprise only |
| Negative review risk | High visibility | High trust impact | RFP impact |

| ARR | FTE hours/month | Budget notes |
|---|---|---|
| <$3M | 4–8 hrs | G2 free tier + manual asks |
| $3–10M | 8–16 hrs | G2 intent optional |

---

## Shared principles (all platforms)

1. Ask at **milestone moments** — not annual blasts
2. Never violate ToS with cash-for-review
3. Respond to negatives **<48h**
4. Keep profiles updated **quarterly**
5. Route inbound with UTM + CRM source fields (`crm-toolkit`)
