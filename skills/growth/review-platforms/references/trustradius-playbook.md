# TrustRadius Playbook

TrustRadius favors **long-form, verified** reviews from real users — strong for enterprise technical evaluators and security-conscious buyers.

---

## Profile optimization

- Complete product profile: integrations, deployment options, supported languages
- Add **comparison pages** vs top 3 competitors
- Technical documentation links (API, SSO, SOC2)
- Screenshots of admin + end-user workflows
- Assign **vendor response owner** (CS or product marketing)

---

## TruVoice & verification

- TrustRadius verifies reviewer employment and usage
- Reviews take **15–30 minutes** — set expectations in ask email
- Incentives: follow current TrustRadius policy (no cash-for-review; charity donations may be allowed — verify ToS)

---

## When to prioritize TrustRadius

| Signal | Action |
|---|---|
| Enterprise deals >$50K ACV | Primary or co-primary with G2 |
| Technical buyer (IT, security, data) | Emphasize depth questions |
| RFP asks for peer references | Export verified quotes |
| G2 reviews feel shallow | Migrate promoters to TrustRadius for depth |

---

## Review generation

**Best ask moments:** post-implementation, security review passed, renewal, executive sponsor praise.

1. CSM identifies promoter (NPS 9–10 or verbal advocate)
2. Send **personal email** with TrustRadius direct link (from vendor portal)
3. Offer to draft bullet points from their words (never ghost-write false claims)
4. Target **1–2 new verified reviews/quarter** for enterprise; quality over volume

---

## Responding to reviews

| Type | Pattern |
|---|---|
| **Positive** | Thank reviewer; cite specific capability they mentioned; invite CS contact |
| **Mixed** | Acknowledge gap; state fix or roadmap without over-promise; offline follow-up |
| **Negative** | <48h response; empathize; factual correction if inaccurate; exec escalation |
| **Competitor / fake** | Flag to TrustRadius; factual vendor reply; internal competitive intel log |

Use `templates/review-response-playbook.md` for tone and structure.

---

## Competitive intel

- Monthly export: competitor review velocity + theme tags
- Tag: implementation, support, pricing, integrations, security
- Feed `positioning-messaging` and `battlecard-builder`
