## Key reference tables

| Source | → Derivative Assets |
|---|---|
| Blog post (2000 words) | LinkedIn post (text-only), LinkedIn carousel (key points), Twitter thread, email newsletter section, podcast talking point, YouTube short, infographic, quotes for social graphics, slide deck, guest post angle |
| Podcast episode | Blog post (transcript + insights), social clips (60s), quote graphics, LinkedIn post, email to subscribers, show notes with links |
| Webinar/video | Blog recap, social clips (30-60s), slide deck, email follow-up to registrants, LinkedIn post with key insight, quotes from speakers |

| Channel | Format | Frequency | Effort |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Newsletter** | Curated content + original insight | 1x/week | Medium |
| **LinkedIn (founder)** | Text post, carousel, video | 3-5x/week | Low (per post) |
| **LinkedIn (company)** | Article share + commentary | 1-2x/week | Low |
| **Twitter/X** | Thread, short post, quote | Daily | Low |
| **Reddit** | Value-first post in relevant subs | 1-2x/week | Medium |
| **Hacker News** | Launch/product posts | As relevant | Medium |
| **Industry communities** | Value posts (no self-promo) | 1-2x/week | Low |
| **Email to subscribers** | New content alert | Per piece | Low |
| **Paid amplification** | Boost top performers | Ongoing | Budget-dependent |
| **Partner cross-promotion** | Mutual sharing | 1-2x/month | Medium |
| **Aggregators (Zest, Refind)** | Submit top pieces | Per piece | Low |
