## Authoritative foundations

- **Meta Creative Testing Framework** — Meta's structured approach to creative testing: test one variable at a time (hook, visual, CTA), run 3-5 variants minimum, 7-14 day test window, kill underperformers, iterate winners. Creative is the highest-leverage variable in paid performance.
- **ARC Ads Creative Methodology** — The Analyze-Research-Create framework for ad creative development. Analyze performance data to identify what's working. Research audience reactions and competitor patterns. Create against a brief, not intuition.
- **Ann Handley — Everybody Writes** — Writing quality matters in ad creative as much as visuals. Handley's principles: write for one person, use specific language over clever language, and remove every word that doesn't earn its place.

## Process phases

- **Phase 1: Platform Audit** — Inventory active ad platforms, document current creative formats and performance by platform
- **Phase 2: Testing Plan** — Define test variables, sample sizes, duration, and success metrics per platform
- **Phase 3: Creative Production** — AI-augmented workflow for generating 10-20 testable variants per week
- **Phase 4: Fatigue Management** — Monitor frequency, rotate creative every 2-3 weeks, maintain 3-5 active variants per ad set
