# Social Media Strategy

## Overview
Social media for B2B is not about going viral — it's about being the first
person your ICP thinks of when they have a problem. Every post should build
trust, demonstrate expertise, or start a conversation. This skill covers
platform-specific strategy, content formats, sizing guides, and engagement
tactics for LinkedIn (primary B2B channel) and X/Twitter (secondary).

## Step-by-Step Process
### Phase 1: Platform Selection
| Platform | Best For | Post Cadence | Content Types |
|---|---|---|---|
| **LinkedIn** | B2B thought leadership, trust-building | 3-5x/week | Text posts, carousels, polls, video |
| **X/Twitter** | Real-time commentary, community | 1-3x/day | Threads, short posts, replies |
| **YouTube** | Long-form education, demos | 1-2x/week | Tutorials, interviews, case studies |
| **TikTok/Reels** | Brand awareness, younger audience | 3-5x/week | Short-form video, trends |

### Phase 2: LinkedIn Content Strategy
Content mix: 40% insights from your work, 30% reactions to industry news,
20% personal stories/lessons, 10% promotion. Never: 100% company content.
Best formats: text-only posts (highest reach), document carousels (highest
engagement), polls (fastest growth).

Posting time: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am or 12-1pm local time. Test what
works for your audience.

### Phase 3: Social Media Sizing Guide
**LinkedIn:**
- Profile photo: 400x400px
- Banner: 1584x396px
- Post image: 1200x627px (single), 1080x1080px (square)
- Carousel document: 1080x1080px per slide
- Video: 1080p, under 10 min

**X/Twitter:**
- Profile photo: 400x400px
- Header: 1500x500px
- In-stream image: 1200x675px (16:9)
- Video: 1080p, under 2:20

### Phase 4: Engagement Tactics
Comment on 5-10 ICP-relevant posts before posting your own. Reply to every
comment within 1 hour. DM people who engage consistently. Build relationships
in DMs, not just public posts.

## Common Pitfalls
1. **Company page as primary** — people follow people, not companies. Founder
   and employee profiles outperform company pages 10:1.
2. **Links in LinkedIn posts** — algorithm severely deprioritizes external
   links. Put links in comments or "link in bio."
3. **Inconsistent posting** — posting 5x one week then nothing for 3 weeks
   kills momentum. Consistency > frequency.
4. **Broadcasting, not engaging** — posting without commenting on others'
   content is talking to an empty room.
