## Authoritative foundations

- **Gary Vaynerchuk — Volume of content wins. AI makes volume possible.** — Pre-2025, producing 30 videos/day required a production team. AI avatars (HeyGen/Synthesia) and AI editing (Descript, Captions) make single-person video operations viable. Volume compounds — every video is a lottery ticket for reach.
- **HeyGen/Synthesia — AI spokesperson technology** — Generate professional talking-head videos from text scripts. No camera, no lighting, no retakes. Best for: demo walkthroughs, personalized outreach at scale, training content, multi-language video localization.
- **Runway — Generative AI video** — Text-to-video and video-to-video generation. Best for: B-roll generation, background replacement, style transfer, concept videos. Not yet production-ready for final output — use for concepting and rough cuts.
- **Opus Clip — AI-powered video clipping** — Automatically identifies the most engaging moments in long-form video and clips them into short-form vertical content. One 30-minute webinar → 8-12 TikTok/Reels/Shorts-ready clips.
- **Descript — AI-first video editing** — Edit video by editing text. Remove filler words with one click. AI voice cloning for corrections. Studio Sound for noise removal. Makes video editing as fast as document editing.
