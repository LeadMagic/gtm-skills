# Deal Review Guide — MEDDICC (Andy Whyte)

**Same scorecard as** `pipeline-management` and `meeting-prep` — managers inspect evidence, not confidence.

## Scorecard (0 / 1 / 2 per dimension, max 14)

| Dim | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **M** Metrics | None | Our ROI only | Buyer-validated metrics |
| **E** Economic Buyer | Unknown | Identified, no access | Direct access + met |
| **D** Decision Criteria | Unknown | Partial | Documented + aligned |
| **D** Decision Process | Unknown | Steps named | Dates + owners per step |
| **I** Identify Pain | Generic | Validated pain | Quantified impact |
| **C** Champion | None | Interested contact | Passes 4-part test |
| **C** Competition | Unknown | Named | Influence on criteria |

## Champion Four-Part Test

1. Power to influence decision  
2. Personal win defined  
3. Can articulate pain without you  
4. Actively selling internally  

Coach rep to **prove** each with evidence (email, meeting note, doc).

## Stage Gate Minimums (consultative — verify in pipeline-management)

| Stage | Min score | Required dims |
|---|---:|---|
| Discovery complete | 6/14 | Pain ≥1, Champion ≥1 |
| Proposal | 10/14 | Champion=2, EB≥1 |
| Commit forecast | 13/14 | EB=2, Process=2 |

## 15-Minute Deal Review Flow

1. **Rep:** State stage, amount, close date, next step (30 sec)
2. **Rep:** Walk scorecard — cite evidence per 2 (5 min)
3. **Manager:** Challenge lowest dimension (5 min)
4. **Action plan:** Max 3 actions with dates (3 min)
5. **Forecast:** Confirm or downgrade category (2 min)

## Coaching Phrases (not "close harder")

- "You scored Champion 2 — show me the internal email they sent."
- "EB is 0 — who owns budget and when are we meeting them?"
- "Critical Event in SPICED is stale — what's the real deadline?"
- "Competition includes status quo — what's the cost of no decision?"

## Multi-Thread Coaching

If single-threaded: assign `multi-thread-orchestration` actions before advancing stage.
