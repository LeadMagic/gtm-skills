# JOLT Coaching — Manager Guide

For deals with solution fit but **indecision** — load `buyer-indecision` for full playbook.

## When to Coach JOLT (not MEDDICC harder)

- MEDDICC ≥ stage gate but no signature  
- Champion says "need to think about it"  
- Multiple stakeholders looping with no decision  
- Rep keeps sending more content — velocity drops  

## Manager Coaching Prompts by JOLT Step

### J — Judge indecision

> "On 1–10, how indecis is the buyer? What evidence — not gut?"  
> "Is this FOMU (fear of messing up) or true objection?"

Coach rep to score in CRM field or deal note.

### O — Offer recommendation

> "What explicit recommendation did you make on the last call?"  
> "If you were them, what would you buy and why?"

Coach: one recommended package — not three options.

### L — Limit exploration

> "How many more eval paths are open? What can we close?"  
> "What did we agree to stop comparing?"

Coach: collapse RFP sprawl; limit proof requests.

### T — Take risk off the table

> "What risk reversal did we offer — pilot, opt-out, SLA, success plan?"  
> "Who from our exec team sponsored their exec?"

Coach: specific risk removal — not bigger discount alone.

## Manager Anti-Coaching (don't say)

- "Send another case study"  
- "Add more ROI slides"  
- "Discount to close" (without JOLT diagnosis)  
- "Push harder"
