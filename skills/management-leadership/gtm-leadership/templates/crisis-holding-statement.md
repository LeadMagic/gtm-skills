# Crisis Holding Statement Template

*Use within 30–60 minutes of confirmed incident. Legal review required Sev 3+.*

**Not legal advice.** Replace bracketed fields. Do not admit liability without counsel.

---

## Outage / service degradation

**Status page / social (short)**

> We are aware of [issue — e.g., elevated errors / partial outage] affecting [product area]. Our engineering team is investigating. We will provide updates every [30/60] minutes at [status page URL]. Customers with urgent needs: [support email or link].

**Company LinkedIn / X (optional)**

> [Company] is investigating [brief description]. Status updates: [URL]. We apologize for the disruption to your work.

---

## Security incident (suspected — no scope admission)

> We are investigating a security matter that may affect [product/service]. We have activated our incident response process and engaged [internal team / external experts]. Out of caution, we recommend customers [specific action if counsel-approved — e.g., rotate API keys / review account activity]. We will share more information as our investigation allows. Questions: [security@ or support@].

**Do not include:** Number of records, attacker attribution, or "breach confirmed" until counsel approves.

---

## General / media inquiry

> We are aware of [reports/questions regarding X]. We take this seriously and are reviewing the situation. We will comment further when we have verified information. Media: [press@].

---

## Internal checklist before publish

- [ ] Eng liaison confirmed facts in war room doc
- [ ] No fix ETA unless eng signed off
- [ ] Legal skim (Sev 3+)
- [ ] Status page and social copy match
- [ ] Support team has FAQ: `crisis-faq-for-support.md`
