# Termination Conversation Guide — [Name]

**⚠️ Run with HR present. Consult `employment-compliance` before delivery.**

**Date/time:** [Scheduled] · **Location:** [Private room / video] · **Duration:** 10–15 min max

## Before Meeting

- [ ] Termination letter / severance docs ready
- [ ] System access revocation scheduled (same hour)
- [ ] Pipeline reassignment owner named
- [ ] Team communication draft ready
- [ ] Equipment return plan

## Script (Manager)

**Direct opening:**
> "[Name], I've asked you here because we've made the decision to end your employment with [Company], effective today."

**Brief reason (factual):**
> "This decision follows [the performance plan we discussed / repeated missed targets / role elimination]. We documented this on [dates]."

**Do not:** Argue, negotiate performance, or discuss other employees.

**Logistics:**
> "HR will walk you through severance, benefits, and equipment return. [HR name] is here now."

**Close:**
> "I wish you well. HR has the details."

## After Meeting

- [ ] CRM opps reassigned within 24h
- [ ] Team notified same day (manager + HR)
- [ ] Customers transitioned per account plan
- [ ] No public commentary on individual

## Team Communication (Draft)

> "Today [Name] is no longer with [Company]. [Interim owner] will own their accounts effective immediately. Direct questions to me."
