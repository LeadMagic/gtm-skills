# Crisis Customer Email Template

*For affected customer segment — Sev 2+. Personalize [brackets]. Legal review Sev 3+.*

**Not legal advice.**

---

## Subject line options

- `Important update about [Product] — [brief issue]`
- `We're addressing an issue affecting [feature/service]`
- `[Company] incident update — [date]`

---

## Email body

**Subject:** [choose above]

Dear [Customer name / team],

We want to inform you directly about an issue that [may have affected / is affecting] your use of [product/feature].

**What happened**  
[2–3 sentences. Facts only. No blame. Example: "On [date] at [time UTC], we detected elevated error rates in [API / dashboard / integration]. Some customers experienced [timeouts / failed syncs / inability to log in]."]

**What we're doing**  
[Concrete actions: "Our engineering team [identified the cause / is actively investigating]. We have [deployed a fix / rolled back a change / scaled capacity]. We expect [next update at TIME] via our status page at [URL]."]

**What you can do**  
[Optional — counsel-approved. Example: "If you see failed jobs from [window], you can safely retry after [time]. Contact support if you need help reconciling data."]

**Support**  
Reply to this email or reach us at [support@] / [phone for enterprise]. Reference incident ID: [INC-XXX].

We apologize for the disruption and appreciate your patience.

[Name]  
[Title — CEO for Sev 3+, or VP CS / Head of Support]  
[Company]

---

## ACKNOWLEDGE → OWN → ACT → FOLLOW UP

| Element | This template section |
|---|---|
| Acknowledge | What happened |
| Own | Apologize; company voice |
| Act | What we're doing + what you can do |
| Follow up | Status page + incident ID + next update |

---

## Segmentation

| Segment | Customize |
|---|---|
| Enterprise | Offer CSM call; mention SLA credit process if applicable |
| SMB | Shorter; link to status page + help center |
| Free / trial | Status page only unless widespread |
