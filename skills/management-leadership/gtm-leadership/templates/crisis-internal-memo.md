# Crisis Internal Memo Template

*Send to all employees before or simultaneous with external holding statement.*

---

## Subject

`Internal: [Incident name] — what we know and how to help`

---

## Memo body

Team,

We are managing an active incident regarding **[one-line description]**. This memo is the official internal summary — please do not post about this on personal social media until we communicate externally.

### What we know (as of [timestamp])

- [Fact 1]
- [Fact 2]
- [What we do **not** know yet]

### Severity

[Sev 1 / 2 / 3 / 4] — [one sentence on customer/revenue impact]

### Your role

| If you are… | Do this |
|---|---|
| **Engineering** | Focus on war room channel [#incident]. No ad-hoc updates to customers. |
| **CS / Support** | Use FAQ: [link to crisis-faq-for-support.md]. Escalate angry execs to [CS captain]. |
| **Sales** | Do not speculate with prospects. Use talk track in FAQ. Pause sequences to [affected domains] if directed. |
| **Marketing** | Pause scheduled social and email campaigns until comms lead clears. |
| **Everyone else** | Direct external questions to [comms@ or support@]. |

### External communications

- **Customers:** [status page / email — timing]
- **Investors:** [if applicable — timing]
- **Press:** [all inquiries to press@ — do not respond individually]

### Next update

We will update this channel by **[time]** or sooner if material facts change.

Thank you for staying focused on [protecting customers / resolving the issue].

[CEO or Comms lead name]

---

## Manager talking points (optional attach)

1. Acknowledge stress; point to official channel only.
2. No guessing root cause in customer calls.
3. Report new customer impact examples to CS captain.
