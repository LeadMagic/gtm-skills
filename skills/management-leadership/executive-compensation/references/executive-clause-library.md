# Executive Clause Library — GTM Leaders

**Disclaimer:** Template language for negotiation — not legal advice. Require employment counsel before execution.

## 1. Employment Status

**At-Will.** Employment is at-will and may be terminated by either party at any time, with or without cause or notice, subject to severance provisions below.

**Cause Definition.** "Cause" includes: (a) material breach of agreement or policy after written cure period of [10] days; (b) fraud, embezzlement, or willful misconduct; (c) conviction of felony; (d) material failure to perform duties after written notice and [30]-day cure; (e) violation of code of conduct. Excludes failure to meet quota absent willful neglect.

## 2. Base Salary & Variable Compensation

**Base.** $[X] annually, paid semi-monthly per company payroll.

**Target Variable.** Target annual incentive $[Y] ([Z]% of base + variable = OTE $[OTE]).

**Performance Period.** Fiscal year [Jan 1 – Dec 31], subject to board-approved plan.

**Metrics & Weights.**

| Metric | Weight | Target | Floor |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Net new ARR | [45]% | $[X] | [80]% of target |
| NRR | [25]% | [110]% | [105]% |
| EBITDA / Rule of 40 | [20]% | [score] | [floor] |
| Org milestones | [10]% | Per exhibit | [list] |

**Interpolation.** Payout between floor and target linear unless board approves curve.

**Maximum Payout.** Variable capped at [150]% of target unless board approves accelerators.

## 3. Clawback & Recoupment

**Logo Clawback.** If a customer logo credited to executive's period churns or downgrades within [90] days of close, [100]% of variable attributed to that logo is recouped from future payouts.

**Financial Restatement.** If company restates financials due to executive fraud or gross negligence, board may recoup variable and equity gains per company recoupment policy.

**Overpayment.** Company may offset future variable for documented overpayment errors.

## 4. Equity

**Grant.** Option/RSU grant of [X] shares ([Y]% fully diluted), subject to board approval and equity plan.

**Vest.** [4] years monthly/quarterly after [1]-year cliff.

**Refresh.** Eligible for annual refresh up to [25]% of initial grant subject to performance review.

**Termination.** Unvested forfeited on termination. Vested exercise window [90] days post-termination (negotiate longer for exec).

## 5. Change of Control

**Single Trigger.** [Not recommended — strike if requested.]

**Double Trigger.** If termination without cause or constructive termination within [12] months of change of control, [100]% of unvested equity accelerates.

**Definition.** Change of control = sale of >50% voting securities, merger, or asset sale constituting substantially all assets.

## 6. Severance

**Without Cause (Company).** [6] months base salary continuation + [pro-rata / 50%] target variable + [COBRA] months + [6] months accelerated vest (if double-trigger not met).

**For Cause / Resignation Without Good Reason.** No severance; COBRA at employee cost.

**Good Reason.** Material reduction in OTE, title, or duties; relocation >[50] miles without consent.

**Release.** Severance conditioned on signed release of claims.

## 7. Restrictive Covenants

**Non-Solicit (Employees).** [12] months — may not solicit company employees to leave.

**Non-Solicit (Customers).** [12] months — may not solicit customers/prospects actively sold in last [12] months.

**Non-Compete.** [Jurisdiction-specific — counsel required. Many states void or limit.]

**Confidentiality.** Per company CIIA; survives termination.

## 8. Commission Plan Incorporation

Executive variable governed by this agreement **and** annual Executive Incentive Plan exhibit approved by board. Plan may be amended prospectively with [30] days notice; no retroactive reduction of earned amounts.

## 9. D&O and Indemnification

Company maintains D&O insurance and indemnifies executive to fullest extent permitted by law and charter/bylaws for acts within scope of duties.

## 10. Parachute (280G)

If payments constitute parachute payments under IRC §280G, company will [cut back / gross up / neither] per board policy — specify before signing.

## 11. Signing & Relocation

**Signing Bonus.** $[X], repayable if voluntary departure within [12] months.

**Relocation.** $[X] lump sum or reimbursed per policy if relocation >[50] miles.

## 12. Forecast Certification

Executive certifies reasonable basis for forecasts submitted to board. Material misrepresentation may constitute cause per exhibit.

## 13. Cooperation

Post-termination, executive cooperates [reasonable hours] with transition and litigation at [hourly rate / no additional comp] for [12] months.

## 14. Section 409A

Payments structured to comply with or be exempt from IRC §409A; six-month delay for specified employees if required.

## 15. Dispute Resolution

[Arbitration / litigation] in [state]; prevailing party fees per policy.
