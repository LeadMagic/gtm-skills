# Executive Comp Benchmarks — GTM Leaders

**Last updated:** June 2025 · **Effective:** H1 2026  
**Sources:** Bridge Group leadership surveys, **Sam Jacobs / Pavilion CRO Council** (member directional), Betts Comp Engine, OpenView executive comp commentary.

Directional US B2B SaaS — triangulate at offer. Founders must get **board approval** before verbal VP/CRO numbers.

## OTE Bands by ARR

| Company ARR | VP Sales OTE | CRO OTE | Base/Var (CRO) |
|---|---|---|---|
| $3–10M | $240–320K | N/A (VP scope) | 60/40 |
| $10–30M | $300–400K | $420–580K | 65/35 |
| $30–75M | $340–480K | $520–720K | 60/40 |
| $75M+ | — | $620–950K+ | 55/45 – 60/40 |

## Equity (Initial Grant)

| Role | % Fully Diluted | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| VP Sales (first) | 0.25–1.0% | Higher if pre-$5M ARR |
| CRO (first) | 0.5–2.0% | Board + investor approval |
| CRO (replacement) | 0.3–1.0% | Lower if late-stage |

## Variable Gate Prevalence (Scale Stage)

| Metric | % of companies using (indicative) |
|---|---|
| Revenue / ARR | ~95% |
| NRR / GRR | ~60% (rising) |
| EBITDA / margin | ~40% |
| Bookings only | declining |

## Severance Market

| Role | Without cause (typical) |
|---|---|
| VP Sales | 3–6 mo base + partial bonus |
| CRO | 6–12 mo base + 50–100% target bonus |

## Signing Bonus

| Situation | Range |
|---|---|
| Relocation | $25–75K |
| Lost bonus at prior co | 50–100% of forfeited bonus |
| Competitive bid | $50–150K (repayable 12 mo) |

## Anti-Patterns

- CRO at $8M ARR with $600K OTE — burns runway, attracts mercenaries
- No NRR gate at consumption/pricing model with downgrade risk
- 0% equity for first VP Sales — loses to funded competitors
