# Onboarding Questions — Both Sides

*Experts: **Mark Roberge** (onboarding machine — measure ramp inputs), **Kevin Dorsey** (manager coaching cadence), **Jacco van der Kooij / WbD** (REKS week 3), **Jon Barrows** (skill certification).*

---

## Manager → new hire (Week 1)

### Day 1 — expectations

1. What does success look like in your first 30 / 60 / 90 days — in your own words?
2. What part of the role are you most confident about? Most nervous about?
3. How do you learn best — shadowing, docs, drills, feedback loops?
4. What did your last manager do that helped you ramp fast? What hurt?
5. Any constraints we should know — timezone, caregiving, travel limits?

### Day 3 — tools & motion

6. Walk me through your understanding of our ICP — who we sell to and who we disqualify.
7. Which pipeline stage is least clear? (Fix in CRM lab.)
8. Do you have everything you need to shadow calls this week? What's missing?
9. What questions do you have about quota, territory, and lead sources?

### Week 2 — certification prep

10. Record a practice opener — what feedback do you want from me?
11. Which Gong calls should you study first? (Manager assigns 3.)
12. REKS self-check: Results / Effort / Knowledge / Skills — where do you feel weakest?

### Day 30 / 60 / 90 (recurring)

| Checkpoint | Manager asks |
|---|---|
| **30** | "What would you do differently if you restarted day 1?" |
| **30** | "Are attainment expectations clear and fair?" |
| **60** | "What's blocking 50% quota — skill, data, or focus?" |
| **90** | "What should we change in onboarding for the next hire?" |

## New hire → company (ask in Week 1)

*Strong hires interview the company — not just the reverse. Candidates: use in offer stage too (`gtm-recruiting`).*

### Role & quota

1. How is quota set — bottom-up or top-down? What % of team hit last year?
2. What is average ramp time to full quota for this role?
3. Territory / list source — inbound, outbound, partners? Who builds lists?
4. What's the SQO / meeting definition? Who qualifies?

### Enablement & coaching

5. What does week 1 look like hour-by-hour?
6. How often will my manager coach me in weeks 1–4?
7. Where is the playbook — messaging, objections, demo flow?
8. Certification before live sends — yes or no?

### Tools & data

9. CRM hygiene expectations — required fields per stage?
10. Sequencer, enrichment, Gong — what's the stack? Any tool gaps?
11. How is marketing supporting pipeline — MQL quality, content, events?

### Culture & growth

12. How do wins get celebrated? (Slack norms — `slack-engagement.md`)
13. Path from this role to next — timeline and criteria?
14. Why did the last person in this seat leave?
15. What would you change about the sales org if you could?

### Red flags if answers are vague

| Vague answer | Risk |
|---|---|
| "Quota is achievable if you work hard" | No attainment data |
| "We don't really onboard — sink or swim" | Ramp failure |
| "CRM is a mess but we're fixing it" | Hygiene tax on rep |
| "We can't share team attainment" | Hidden comp/quota issue |

## HR / People Ops (Day 0)

- Benefits, payroll, PTO — who is your HR contact?
- Security training deadline before production data?
- Who approves expenses and customer travel?

## Buddy program questions (peer)

**Buddy asks new hire:**
- What's confusing that you won't ask the manager?
- Want to shadow my calls tomorrow?

**New hire asks buddy:**
- What do you wish you'd known week 1?
- Who are the best internal helpers for deal desk, RevOps, marketing?
