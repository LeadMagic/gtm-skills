# Ramp Benchmarks — Revenue Roles

*Last updated: June 2025 / H1 2026 — directional bands for US B2B SaaS.*

Ramp is measured from **start date**, not offer acceptance. Adjust ±20% for enterprise cycle length or PLG-assisted inbound.

---

## Time-to-productivity

| Role | First qualified meeting | First opp created | 50% quota | Full quota | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **SDR (outbound)** | 14–21 days | 30–45 days (SQO) | Day 60 | Day 90 | Certify messaging before dial |
| **SDR (inbound)** | 7–14 days | 21–30 days | Day 45 | Day 75 | Faster list access |
| **AE (SMB)** | 21–30 days | 30–45 days | Day 60 | Day 90 | Self-source 2 opps by day 30 |
| **AE (mid-market)** | 30–45 days | 45–60 days | Day 75 | Day 120 | Longer shadow path |
| **AE (enterprise)** | 45–60 days | 60–90 days | Day 90 | Day 150+ | Multi-thread cert |
| **Sales manager** | N/A | N/A | Day 90 | Day 120 | Team forecast accuracy |

## 90-day attainment

| Motion | Target vs tenured peer | Red flag |
|---|---|---|
| Outbound SDR | ≥70% of median SQOs | <50% |
| Inbound SDR | ≥75% | <55% |
| AE (any) | ≥70% of median bookings | <50% |
| Manager | Team at ≥85% of forecast accuracy | <70% |

---

## Onboarding system levers

| Lever | Typical impact |
|---|---|
| Day 0 CRM + sequencer access | −2 weeks to first meeting |
| Buddy program (4 weeks) | +15–25% 90-day attainment |
| Daily manager coaching weeks 1–4 | +10–20% certification pass rate |
| Pre-built lists in CRM day 1 | −1–2 weeks idle time |
| Gong top-call library | Faster discovery pattern match |
| Certification before live send | Fewer brand / compliance incidents |

## REKS ramp diagnostic (week 3)

From Winning by Design — run via `sales-coaching`:

| REKS gap | Ramp symptom | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| **Results** | Activity without meetings | List quality + ICP drill |
| **Effort** | Low activity | Coaching cadence + accountability |
| **Knowledge** | Bad discovery scores | Enablement + shadowing |
| **Skills** | Objection failures | Barrows-style rep drills |
