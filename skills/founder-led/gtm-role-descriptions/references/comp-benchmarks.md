## Core Ratios (Bridge Group)

| Metric | Benchmark | Red Flag |
|---|---|---|
| Quota : OTE | ~5:1 | <3:1 (quota too low) or >7:1 (unrealistic) |
| OTE : Year-1 quota | ≤20% | >25% (overpaid for output) |
| AE ramp | 4.5–6 months full productivity | >9 months without enablement fix |
| SDR : AE ratio | 2:1 outbound-heavy; 1:1 inbound-heavy | 4:1+ without manager |
| Manager span | 6–8 reps | >10 without second-line |

## Commission Mechanics (AE)

**Standard:**
- 100% attainment = 100% variable
- Linear 0–100%; accelerators 100–120% (1.5×); 120%+ (2×)
- Clawback: 90 days on churned deals (state in offer letter)
- Draw: recoverable months 1–3 optional for first AE hire

**President's Club:** Top 10–20% at ≥110–120% attainment; budget 1–2% of sales payroll.

## What Candidates Check (RepVue / Compgauge)

Before posting, ensure internal reality matches external perception:
- OTE vs actual earnings (not just posted range)
- Quota attainability (% of team at quota)
- Ramp support and enablement
- Tech stack and lead flow
- Manager quality

Misalignment → high apply volume, low accept rate, bad Glassdoor/RepVue scores.
