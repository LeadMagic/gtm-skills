# SaaS GTM Compensation Strategies

**Last updated:** June 2025 · **Bands:** H1 2026

Sources: Bridge Group, Winning by Design, Sam Jacobs / Pavilion CRO Council,
Mark Roberge, RepVue, Betts Comp Engine.

**Canonical strategy:** `executive-compensation/references/gtm-compensation-strategy.md`.  
**Bands:** `references/comp-benchmarks.md`. **Founders:** `founder-comp-playbook`.

## Core Principles

| Principle | Benchmark | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Quota : OTE | ~5:1 (IC) | Rep can earn at 100%; company unit economics work |
| OTE : quota | ≤20% | Overpaying for under-delivery |
| Pay for outcomes | Not activity alone | SDR: SQO; CSM: NRR; AE: ARR with clawback |
| Ramp protects | 25–75% Q1–Q2 | New hire shouldn't miss through no fault |
| Transparency | Publish ranges | RepVue-era candidates compare before apply |

## IC Strategies by Role

### SDR / BDR

| Model | Structure | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **SQO-primary** | 50/50; $/qualified meeting + SQO bonus | Outbound quality |
| **Hybrid** | 60/40; meetings + opp created | Blended inbound/outbound |
| **Promotion path** | Documented AE track 12–18 mo | Retention |

**Avoid:** Pure dial/call volume — pays spam.

### AE (Mid-Market)

| Model | Structure | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Linear 50/50** | 100% attainment = 100% variable | Steady state |
| **Accelerated** | 1.5× 100–120%; 2× 120%+ | Aggressive growth |
| **Draw (first hire)** | Recoverable months 1–3 | Founder transition only |

**Clawback:** 90 days logo churn or downgrade.

### Enterprise AE

| Model | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Split credit** | SDR + AE + SE documented in CRM |
| **Multi-year** | SPIFF or kicker on 2+ year contracts |
| **President's Club** | Top 10–20% at ≥110% |

### Manager (Player-Coach)

| Component | Typical |
|---|---|
| Personal patch | 30–50% IC quota |
| Team override | 5–15% on team attainment |
| OTE | $160–220K |

## Leadership Comp (VP / CRO)

| Stage | OTE Band | Base/Var | Gates |
|---|---|---|---|
| VP Sales | $240–380K | 50/50 or 60/40 | Org ARR + hiring milestones |
| CRO (growth) | $420–650K | 65/35 | ARR + NRR + efficiency |
| CRO (scale) | $500–800K+ | 60/40 | ARR + NRR + EBITDA floor |

**Pavilion trend:** CRO variable gated on **revenue quality** — NRR, EBITDA —
not bookings alone. Prevents mercenary quarter-end deals.

## SPIFFs and Bonuses

| SPIFF Type | When | Amount Guidance |
|---|---|---|
| New logo blitz | Q1 pipeline gap | $500–2K per logo (IC) |
| Multi-year | Enterprise push | 2–5% of TCV one-time |
| Product launch | New SKU | 30-day accelerator on SKU ARR |
| Event follow-up | Conference | $/meeting held in 14 days |

Document SPIFFs separately — don't muddy base plan.

## Comp Plan Approval Checklist (Leaders)

- [ ] Quota:OTE ratio documented
- [ ] Ramp schedule in offer letter
- [ ] Clawback and draw terms clear
- [ ] SDR definitions (qualified meeting) written
- [ ] Manager team + patch quota math shown
- [ ] RevOps can report attainment monthly
- [ ] Legal reviewed for state comp disclosure (`employment-compliance`)

## Anti-Patterns

1. **Quota inflation** — Unreachable quota = turnover + sandbagging culture.
2. **Uncapped accelerators** — Bad-fit mega-deals that churn.
3. **CSM on tickets** — Pays support volume, not NRR.
4. **VP hired on hero number** — No system metrics → CRO churn cycle.
5. **Hidden OTE** — RepVue backlash; offer decline rate spikes.
