# Revenue Org Structure by ARR Stage

Aligns with `sales-team-building` hire sequence and Winning by Design POD design.

## $0–$500K ARR — Founder-Led

```
CEO/Founder (selling)
├── [Optional] Contract SDR or founder outbound
└── [Optional] Founder CS / support
```

**Systems:** CRM lite, sequencer, enrichment. One person owns stack.

**Don't hire:** VP Sales, demand gen manager, enablement.

---

## $500K–$1M ARR — First Revenue Hire

```
CEO
└── AE #1 (full-cycle, may do own prospecting)
    └── [Optional] Part-time SDR contractor
```

**Trigger:** Founder closed 10–20 deals; playbook documented.

**Comp note:** First AE often 0.25–0.75% equity premium.

---

## $1M–$3M ARR — POD v0

```
CEO / Head of Sales (player-coach founder)
├── SDR × 1–2
├── AE × 2–3
└── CSM × 0–1 (if NRR risk)
```

**POD ratio target:** 2 SDR : 2 AE : 0–1 CSM (outbound-heavy).

**Add RevOps** when CRM breaks forecasting (usually $2M+).

---

## $3M–$10M ARR — Managed PODs

```
CRO or VP Sales
├── Sales Manager (player-coach) OR Director
│   ├── SDR Team Lead
│   │   └── SDR × 3–6
│   └── AE × 4–8
├── CS Manager
│   └── CSM × 2–4
├── RevOps × 1
└── [Optional] Sales Enablement × 1
```

**POD ratio:** 2:1 SDR:AE per pod; 1 CSM per $1–2M ARR managed.

**Marketing alignment:**
```
CMO / Head of Marketing
└── Demand Gen × 1–2  →  feeds SDR inbound queue
```

---

## $10M–$30M ARR — Functional Leaders

```
CRO
├── VP Sales
│   ├── Enterprise AE team
│   ├── Mid-market AE team
│   └── SDR org (Manager + ICs)
├── VP Customer Success
│   ├── CSM pods by segment
│   └── Support / Onboarding
├── RevOps (2–4)
│   ├── Systems / CRM
│   ├── Analytics / Forecasting
│   └── Deal desk (if enterprise)
├── Sales Enablement
└── [Partnership with] VP Marketing
```

**Span of control:** Managers at 6–8 reps. Second-line at 15+ reps.

---

## $30M+ ARR — Full Revenue Engine

```
CRO
├── SVP / VP Sales (New Business)
├── SVP / VP Customer Success (NRR owner)
├── VP Revenue Operations
├── VP Sales Enablement
├── VP Partnerships / Alliances [optional]
└── Chief of Staff to CRO [optional]
```

**Board metrics:** ARR, NRR, CAC payback, magic number, LTV:CAC, quota attainment distribution.

## POD Design (Winning by Design)

| POD Element | Ratio | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| SDR → AE | 2:1 outbound | 1:1 if inbound-heavy |
| AE → CSM | 1:50–100 accounts or $1–2M ARR/CSM | Segment by tier |
| Manager → IC | 1:6–8 | Player-coach until 2 managers |

**When to split pods:** AE utilization >85%; SDR meeting surplus; CSM health scores declining.

---

## RevOps Placement

| Stage | RevOps Reports To | Why |
|---|---|---|
| $1–5M | CEO or Head of Sales | Neutral data owner |
| $5–15M | CRO | Cross-functional GTM |
| $15M+ | VP RevOps under CRO | Scale and systems |

---

## Common Org Mistakes

1. **VP Sales before 2 AEs at quota** — hire player-coach AE instead.
2. **CSM before 30+ customers** — founder/onboarding still works.
3. **Demand gen before outbound/inbound math done** — burns cash.
4. **RevOps under Sales** — forecast bias; prefer CRO dotted line.
5. **No enablement with 90+ day ramp** — hidden tax on every hire.
