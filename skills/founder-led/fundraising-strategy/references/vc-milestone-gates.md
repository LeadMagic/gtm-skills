# VC Milestone Gates

What metrics unlock each round. **Jason Lemkin (SaaStr):** 3x at seed; burn discipline at scale. **Christoph Janz (Point Nine):** ARR napkin to unicorn. **David Skok:** unit economics before scale. Threshold conflicts vs Meritech/KeyBanc → `references/benchmark-reconciliation.md`.

## Round Gates Overview

| Round | Typical ARR | Raise | Dilution | Growth gate | Efficiency gate |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-seed | $0–500K | $500K–2M | 10–15% | PMF / retention signal | N/A |
| Seed | $500K–2M | $2–5M | 15–20% | **3x YoY** or clear inflection | Magic # >0.5; NRR >100% |
| Series A | $1–4M | $5–20M | 20–25% | **2–3x YoY** | Burn mult <2x; payback <15 mo |
| Series B | $5–15M | $20–50M | 15–20% | **2x+ YoY** | Burn mult <1.5x; NRR >110% |
| Series C+ | $15M+ | $50M+ | 10–20% | 80%+ → 50%+ decay | Rule of 40 ≥35 trending 40 |

## Pre-Seed → Seed

**Must show:**

- [ ] Retention: NRR >100% or GRR >85% with expansion path
- [ ] Unit economics: LTV:CAC >3x on early cohort (Skok)
- [ ] TAM story credible for $100M+ ARR (Lemkin venture test)
- [ ] Founder closes 10–20 deals (repeatable pain, not one-off)

**Nice to have:** Logo churn <3% monthly; gross margin >70%

**Do not raise if:** CAC payback >24 mo with no fix; NRR <95%

## Seed → Series A

**Must show:**

- [ ] **3x revenue** in 12–18 mo from seed close (or clear line to it)
- [ ] Magic number >0.75 for 2 consecutive quarters
- [ ] NRR >105%; enterprise motion >110% aspirational
- [ ] Sales machine started (Roberge): documented process, first non-founder closes
- [ ] Burn multiple <2x (improving trend)

**Hiring gate:** VP Sales ~$2M ARR (Lemkin — 70% fail earlier)

**Data room:** Cohort retention, ARR bridge, fully loaded CAC

## Series A → Series B

**Must show:**

- [ ] 2x+ YoY at $5M+ ARR
- [ ] NRR >110%; GRR >88%
- [ ] CAC payback <15 mo (enterprise <18)
- [ ] Burn multiple <1.5x
- [ ] VP Sales + RevOps; founder <40% of new ARR
- [ ] Rule of 40 >30% or credible path

**Red flags:** Decelerating 3 quarters; single channel >60% of pipeline

## Series B → C / Growth

**Must show:**

- [ ] Category position (#1 or #2 with wedge)
- [ ] Rule of 40 ≥35; trending 40+
- [ ] Burn multiple <1x preferred
- [ ] International or second product optional but valued
- [ ] Board-ready metrics monthly (`investor-updates`)

## Bootstrap → First Institutional (Optional)

**Bootstrap first, raise later path:**

- $1–2M ARR on customer revenue
- Profitable or near-profitable
- Raise at **lower dilution** (10–15% vs 20%+ seed default)
- Same unit economics gates as seed

Cross-ref: `saas-outcomes/references/bootstrap-vs-vc-paths.md`

## Metrics at Fingertips (Investor Meeting)

From `fundraising-strategy` + `saas-metrics-calculator`:

1. MRR/ARR + 6 mo trend
2. NRR, GRR (cohort definition stated)
3. CAC payback, LTV:CAC (fully loaded)
4. Magic number, burn multiple
5. Gross margin, runway months
6. Logo churn monthly

## When Not to Raise

| Condition | Action |
|---|---|
| Burn multiple >3x without acceleration | Fix efficiency |
| NRR <100% | Fix expansion/churn |
| No $100M path and VC on cap table | Consider bootstrap path |
| Raising for runway only (<9 mo left) | Bridge or cut burn first |
