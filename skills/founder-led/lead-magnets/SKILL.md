# Lead Magnets

## Overview
A lead magnet trades value for contact information. The best lead magnets solve an immediate, specific problem for your ICP — so useful they would pay for it, so quick they consume it in minutes. This skill brainstorms, builds, and distributes lead magnets that convert.

## When to Use
- "Create a lead magnet"
- "Build a free tool for lead gen"
- "What should we gate?"
- "Generate leads with content"

## Step-by-Step Process
### Phase 1: Magnet Ideation
Best formats: calculators (ROI, savings, pricing), templates (spreadsheets, docs, decks), checklists, benchmark reports, mini-courses, free tools. Worst: ebooks (nobody reads them), whitepapers (too slow), webinar recordings (low perceived value).

### Phase 2: Build
Make it genuinely useful. A calculator that gives a real answer. A template that saves hours. If you would not use it yourself, prospects will not either.

### Phase 3: Distribution
Landing page → form (email only) → deliver immediately → nurture sequence. Promote via content, social, partner channels, paid ads.

## Output Format
Lead magnet strategy with ideas, build specs, landing page copy, and distribution plan.

## Common Pitfalls
1. **Gating something nobody wants** — ask "would I give my email for this?"
2. **Too many form fields** — email only. Ask for more later.
3. **Delayed delivery** — send the magnet immediately after form submission.
