# Solo Founder GTM

## Overview
Solo founders do not need a sales team. They need a system. This skill builds lean GTM motions that scale with revenue — tool stacks at each stage, AI agents replacing headcount, and clear triggers for when to make the first hire. Built for bootstrappers and founders doing their own selling.

## When to Use
- "How do I do GTM as a solo founder?"
- "Build my sales stack on a bootstrap budget"
- "When should I hire my first salesperson?"
- "What tools do I need at $X MRR?"
- "Replace SDR work with AI"

## Step-by-Step Process
### Phase 1: Stage Assessment
Score your GTM maturity (WbD GTM Index 1-10 self-assessment). Identify which revenue stage you are in.

### Phase 2: Tool Stack by Stage
**$0-10K MRR**: Apollo ($59/mo for data+sequencing), calendar tool, email. Total: ~$100/mo.
**$10-50K MRR**: Add verification service, CRM (HubSpot free), LinkedIn Sales Nav. Total: ~$250/mo.
**$50K+ MRR**: Add Clay ($149+/mo), Smartlead/Instantly, n8n. Total: ~$500/mo.

### Phase 3: AI Agents as Org
Before hiring: AI SDR for prospecting, AI for meeting prep, AI for follow-up drafting. One founder + AI agents can outproduce a 3-person team.

### Phase 4: Hiring Triggers
Founder closed 10-20 deals personally → hire Senior AE (full-stack). 2 AEs at quota → hire manager. $2M+ ARR → VP Sales. Never hire VP Sales before $2M ARR (70% failure rate).

## Output Format
Stage-based GTM playbook with tool stack, AI agent configuration, hiring triggers, and bootstrapper economics.

## Common Pitfalls
1. **Hiring before the motion works** — founder must personally close 10-20 deals first.
2. **Buying enterprise tools early** — $25K ZoomInfo at $500K ARR is capital misallocation.
3. **SDR as first hire** — hire a full-stack AE who can prospect AND close.
4. **No AI leverage** — one founder + AI agents outperforms 3 junior hires at lower cost.
