# Founder Brand

## Overview
A founder with a strong personal brand wins deals before the first meeting. Prospects read your content, trust your expertise, and come to the sales conversation pre-sold. This skill builds a founder brand strategy: platform selection, content cadence, and trust-building content types.

## When to Use
- "Build my personal brand"
- "Grow my LinkedIn presence"
- "Start a founder newsletter"
- "Get on podcasts"
- "Create content as a founder"

## Step-by-Step Process
### Phase 1: Platform Selection
LinkedIn (primary for B2B founders), newsletter (Substack or beehiiv), podcast guesting (secondary), Twitter/X (optional, depends on audience).

### Phase 2: Content Strategy
Post 2-3x/week on LinkedIn. Topics: lessons from building, data from your business, contrarian takes on industry wisdom, customer stories (anonymized). Never: company press releases, generic motivational content, AI-generated posts without personal voice.

### Phase 3: Growth Flywheel
Post → engagement → profile visits → website visits → demo requests. Track the pipeline generated from content, not just vanity metrics.

## Output Format
Founder brand strategy with platform mix, content calendar, voice guidelines, and pipeline attribution model.

## Common Pitfalls
1. **Posting company content only** — people follow people, not companies.
2. **Inconsistent cadence** — posting bursts then silence loses momentum.
3. **No pipeline tracking** — likes do not pay bills. Track content-to-pipeline.
