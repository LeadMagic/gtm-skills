# Exit Potential Scorecard

**Exit optionality** ≠ "sell someday." Score whether a credible transaction is achievable without distracting from building — and which buyer types are realistic.

**Sources:** `exit-metrics-matrix.md`, `valuation-drivers.md`, `buyer-readiness-checklist.md`, Rob Walling, Nathan Latka, Jason Lemkin, David Skok.

---

## When Exit Is Realistic vs Distraction

| Signal | Realistic (pursue optionality) | Distraction (build first) |
|---|---|---|
| ARR | $2M+ strategic; $5M+ PE; $500K+ bootstrap sale if profitable | <$500K without velocity or profit |
| Growth | Stable or accelerating 2+ quarters | Decelerating with no fix — process won't save multiple |
| Retention | NRR ≥100%; GRR ≥85% | NRR <95% — diligence discount |
| Inbound | 2+ strategic conversations or banker interest | Zero inbound; outbound "shopping" |
| Founder | Wants liquidity or succession; modeled after-tax | FOMO from headline multiple only |
| Team | VP layer or playbook without founder on every deal | Founder-only sales at $3M+ ARR |

**Rule:** Scorecard avg **<3.0** → exit is distraction. Score **3.0–3.9** → passive readiness only. Score **≥4.0** → active optionality (data room, relationships).

## Optionality Scorecard (Rate 1–5)

| Dimension | 1 (weak) | 3 (developing) | 5 (strong) | Score |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Revenue quality** | Services >20%; one-time spikes | Mostly subscription; clean bridge | Multi-year; expansion-driven NRR | /5 |
| **Growth rate** | <20% YoY | 20–40% | >40% or profitable 25%+ margin | /5 |
| **NRR / GRR** | NRR <95% | NRR 100–110% | NRR >115%; GRR >90% | /5 |
| **Concentration** | Top customer >25% ARR | Top customer 15–25% | Top customer <10%; top 3 <35% | /5 |
| **Margin / efficiency** | Burn multiple >2.5x; GM <65% | GM 70–75%; burn 1.5–2x | GM >78%; burn <1x or EBITDA 20%+ | /5 |
| **GTM repeatability** | Founder >50% new ARR | AE team; uneven attainment | >60% reps at quota; documented playbook | /5 |
| **Team depth** | Key-person risk | Manager layer | VP Sales + CS; succession plan | /5 |
| **Legal / cap table** | IP gaps; messy prefs | Cleanable in 6 mo | Audit-ready; all IP assigned | /5 |
| **Strategic fit** | Commodity; no unique asset | Niche leader | Unique data/IP; buyer overlap map | /5 |
| **Process readiness** | No metrics pack | Monthly metrics; partial data room | DD pack <30 days old (`due-diligence-metrics-pack.md`) | /5 |

**Average:** _____ / 5.0

| Average | Interpretation | Action |
|---|---|---|
| <3.0 | Low optionality | Fix retention + concentration; no process |
| 3.0–3.9 | Passive optionality | 12-mo readiness plan; inbound only |
| 4.0–4.4 | Active optionality | Data room; strategic relationships |
| ≥4.5 | High optionality | Counsel engaged; model LOI scenarios |

## Buyer Types by Stage

| Buyer | Realistic when | Primary metrics | Typical structure |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Strategic** | $2M+ ARR; product/customer overlap; category heat | Growth, NRR, integration fit | 5–15x ARR; cash + earnout |
| **PE (financial)** | $5M+ ARR; NRR >110%; EBITDA path 20%+ | EBITDA, GRR, burn multiple | 8–12x ARR or 15–25x EBITDA |
| **Bootstrap / SMB** | Profitable; clean books; <$10M ARR | SDE, churn, margin | 3–6x SDE; 4–8x ARR |
| **Acqui-hire** | <$2M ARR; team + tech velocity | Team quality, stack | $1–3M/engineer band |

## Metrics That Unlock Exit Potential

| Metric | Minimum for process | Premium band | Weight by buyer |
|---|---|---|---|
| **NRR** | >100% | >115–120% | Strategic, PE |
| **YoY growth** | >20% | >40% | Strategic, VC follow-on |
| **GRR** | >85% | >92% | PE |
| **Top customer %** | <20% | <10% | All |
| **Gross margin** | >70% | >78% | PE, strategic |
| **CAC payback** | <18 mo | <12 mo | PE, bootstrap |
| **Rule of 40** | N/A early | ≥40 | PE, public comps |
| **Founder % new ARR** | <50% | <30% | All |

## Go / No-Go: Start Exit Process

**Go** when **all** true:

- [ ] Scorecard average ≥4.0
- [ ] 3+ triggers from `saas-outcomes` Phase 5 (growth, inbound, fatigue, consolidation, peak valuation)
- [ ] After-tax sale ≥ NPV of 5-year hold (`financial-modeling`)
- [ ] NRR ≥100% or credible 2-quarter improvement trend
- [ ] No area scored 1 on scorecard

**No-go** when **any** true:

- [ ] Announcing "for sale" without inbound
- [ ] Fixing churn during diligence only
- [ ] Single LOI with no BATNA
- [ ] Founder closes >50% ARR with no succession
