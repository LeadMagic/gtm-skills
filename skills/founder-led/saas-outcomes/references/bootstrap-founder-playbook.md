# Bootstrap Founder Playbook

**GTM-founder lens:** capital efficiency, exit optionality, and when bootstrap is strategic vs default.  
**Not legal or investment advice.** Reconcile multiples with `references/benchmark-reconciliation.md` and `references/meritech-saas-benchmarks.md` — no hype quotes.

Builds on `bootstrap-vs-vc-paths.md`, `solo-founder-gtm`, and `journey-stage-gates.md`.

---

## Bootstrap Sensibility: Three Modes

| Mode | Goal | Success metric | Typical ARR |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Lifestyle bootstrap** | Cash flow + control | Owner earnings, hours/week | $500K–$3M |
| **Exit-oriented bootstrap** | Clean sale in 5–10 yr | EV at close, optionality score | $1–15M |
| **Strategic default** | Can't raise / won't dilute | Profitable growth, survival | Any — but name it |

**Pride vs economics:** Bootstrapping is often framed as virtue. Economically, it wins when **organic CAC + ownership % × exit $** beats **VC dilution × venture outcome probability**. When market is winner-take-all and competitors raise, bootstrap default is **opportunity cost**, not morality.

## When Bootstrap Is Strategic (vs Default)

**Strategic bootstrap** — you choose it because:

- TAM supports $5–30M leader without $100M story (Lemkin venture test fails — that's OK)
- Gross margin >70%, CAC payback <12 mo on founder-led motion (Skok)
- NRR 100–115% — durable, not venture NRR
- You want **optionality**: hold, MicroAcquire sale, or raise from strength later

**Default bootstrap** — capital isn't available or you fear dilution without modeling alternatives. Sensibility: run **bootstrap vs raise memo** (`templates/outcome-memo.md`) before year 3.

**When NOT to bootstrap** — see `solo-founder-gtm/references/when-not-to-scale.md`:

- Winner-take-all category; well-funded competitor blitzing
- 18+ months pre-revenue with capital-intensive GTM
- Network effects require land-grab speed
- Market window closes in <24 months (regulatory, platform shift)

## Capital Efficiency

### Organic CAC

- Bootstrap targets **LTV:CAC ≥3:1** (Skok minimum); organic often **5–10x** — see `benchmark-reconciliation.md`
- Founder-led sales duration: plan **18–36 months** founder as primary closer through $1–2M ARR (Lemkin 10–20 closes before first AE)
- Content + outbound + referral before paid blitz — `solo-founder-gtm` tool tiers

### Tool spend discipline

Load `gtm-spend-management/references/spend-by-stage.md`:

| ARR | GTM spend % ARR (typical) | Bootstrap guardrail |
|---|---|---|
| $0–500K | 15–25% | Defer ABM, intent, enterprise CRM |
| $500K–$2M | 20–35% | No duplicate SEP + MAP |
| $2M–$10M | 25–40% | RevOps owner before new categories |

**Sensibility:** Bootstrap pride sometimes shows up as **$0 tools** (good early) or **$3K/mo stack at $30K MRR** (bad). Cap tools as % of MRR in capital plan.

### Payroll gates

From `founder-comp-playbook` + `solo-founder-gtm`:

- First AE: founder closed 10–20 deals; MRR usually $80K+ (~$1M ARR)
- VP Sales: ~$2M ARR — not before
- Sales payroll ceiling: 15–25% ARR early; 20–30% at $2M+

## Bootstrap Exit Paths

| Path | Profile | Multiple band (indicative) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **MicroAcquire / marketplaces** | $500K–$5M ARR, clean books | 2–5x ARR or SDE-based | Speed 3–6 mo; less earn-out complexity |
| **Strategic tuck-in** | Product fit, $2–20M ARR | 4–10x ARR + earn-out possible | Synergy premium if competitive |
| **PE roll-up** | $3M+ ARR, EBITDA path | 6–12x ARR or 12–20x EBITDA | Needs GTM beyond founder |
| **Hold + distribute** | 20–40% margin | N/A (cash yield) | Walling calm company |

**Reconcile multiples:** Private bootstrap sales often **2–6x ARR** below $2M; **4–8x** with profit and NRR >105%. VC-backed distressed can look similar — **profitability + NRR** pin the number, not "bootstrap" label. See `benchmark-reconciliation.md` § Bootstrap vs VC Multiples.

**Public comps (Meritech):** Use for **aspiration and board narrative** on venture-adjacent bootstrap — not for quoting MicroAcquire LOIs. Public median growth ~17–21% YoY; your buyer is private and segment-specific.

**Earn-out tradeoff:** Bootstrap sales often favor **higher cash at close, smaller earn-out**. If buyer pushes large earn-out on small deal → `exiting-company/references/negotiating-earn-out.md`.

## Bootstrap → Raise → Exit Hybrid

Increasingly common path:

```
Bootstrap to $1–2M ARR (profitable or efficient)
  → TinySeed / Earnest / Founderpath OR small seed
  → Scale to $5–15M ARR
  → Strategic or PE exit OR secondaries
```

**Gates:** `fundraising-strategy/references/vc-milestone-gates.md` — venture still needs **$100M credible path** if you take classic VC. TinySeed/Earnest ≠ same dilution or growth bar.

**Dilution math:** Bootstrap-first to $2M then seed often preserves **15–25% more founder ownership** vs seed at $200K ARR — model in `financial-modeling`.

## Seller Financing & Rollover (High Level)

| Mechanism | Founder view | Push for |
|---|---|---|
| **Seller note** | You lend buyer part of price | Market interest, security, clear subordination |
| **Rollover equity** | Keep upside in NewCo | Understand prefs, drag-along, liquidity timeline |
| **Retention bonus** | Cash for staying | Separate from earn-out performance |

Not a substitute for **cash at close** meeting your floor — see earn-out worksheet.

## Walk-Away Thresholds (Bootstrap Sale)

Before listing or LOI:

| Threshold | Planning question |
|---|---|
| Minimum cash at close | Covers debt, taxes, 12–18 mo runway? |
| Minimum multiple | Below `benchmark-reconciliation` floor without strategic reason? |
| Earn-out at risk | >35% on sub-$5M deal? |
| Customer concentration | Buyer discount >20% for top logo — accept or fix first? |
| Hold alternative | 3-yr DCF beats LOI after tax? |

Score readiness: `exit-potential-scorecard.md`. If <3.0, fix before process.

## Anti-Patterns

| Mistake | Reality | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| "Bootstrap forever" in WTA market | Competitor captures category | Name raise or niche pivot |
| No books / no metrics pack | Latka/MicroAcquire buyers pass | `due-diligence-metrics-pack.md` early |
| Optimizing Twitter ARR | Diligence strips vanity | ARR bridge, cohort NRR |
| Refusing all dilution then selling cheap | Low EV, 100% of small pie | Hybrid raise or hold for profit |
| Copying VC burn on bootstrap salary | Runway cliff | `bootstrap-capital-plan.md` |
