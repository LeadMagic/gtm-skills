# Journey Stage Gates

Founder/company journey: **idea → PMF search → GTM fit → scale → optimize → exit optionality**.

Each stage has parallel tracks (product, GTM, team, capital). **Advance only when go criteria met** on the primary track for your current stage.

**Sources:** Sean Ellis (PMF), Jason Lemkin (Survival → Thrival), Rob Walling (bootstrap path), David Skok (unit economics), `end-goal-matrix.md`, `solo-founder-gtm` (GTM stages).

---

## Stage Overview

| Stage | Primary question | Typical ARR | Duration (indicative) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1. Idea** | Is the problem real and painful? | $0 | Weeks–months |
| **2. PMF search** | Do customers retain and pull? | $0–$500K | 6–18 months |
| **3. GTM fit** | Is the motion repeatable without heroics? | $500K–$2M | 6–12 months |
| **4. Scale** | Can we add capacity without breaking unit economics? | $2M–$10M+ | 12–36 months |
| **5. Optimize** | Are we efficient at current scale? | $5M–$50M+ | Ongoing |
| **6. Exit optionality** | Is a transaction credible without killing the business? | Path-dependent | 12–24 mo prep |

---

## Stage 1: Idea

| Track | Go criteria (≥3 of 4) | No-go (stop / pivot) |
|---|---|---|
| **Product** | 10+ problem interviews; clear ICP hypothesis; MVP scope ≤4 weeks | No willingness to pay signal after 20 conversations |
| **GTM** | Named first 50 accounts; channel hypothesis (inbound vs outbound) | Cannot articulate why buyer acts now |
| **Team** | Founder(s) can build + sell for 12+ months runway | Missing domain or distribution skill with no plan |
| **Capital** | Bootstrap runway or committed pre-seed path named | Raising without problem validation |

**Gate to PMF search:** Written ICP + problem statement + first 5 design partners or LOIs.

---

## Stage 2: PMF Search

| Track | Go criteria | No-go |
|---|---|---|
| **Product** | Sean Ellis ≥40% "very disappointed"; cohort retention flattening | Logo churn >5%/mo SMB after month 3 |
| **GTM** | Pull signals: inbound, referrals, shortened cycle vs first deals | 100% push; no organic or word-of-mouth |
| **Team** | Founder closes; no premature hires | First AE before 10 founder closes |
| **Capital** | Unit economics directionally positive OR VC story with retention proof | Raising to fix churn |

**Gate to GTM fit:** PMF checklist pass → `solo-founder-gtm/references/pmf-signal-checklist.md`. NRR path >100% or GRR >85% with documented ICP.

---

## Stage 3: GTM Fit

| Track | Go criteria | No-go |
|---|---|---|
| **Product** | Core use case stable; roadmap tied to expansion/churn fixes | One customer drives >40% roadmap |
| **GTM** | Founder closed 10–20 deals; documented playbook; CAC payback <18 mo | Founder still only seller at $1M+ ARR with no doc |
| **Team** | WbD GTM Index ≥6 on Operating + Math models | Index <6 and hiring anyway |
| **Capital** | Path matches `end-goal-matrix.md` (bootstrap vs VC) | Mixed playbooks (VC burn, bootstrap ownership) |

**Gate to scale:** `solo-founder-gtm/references/scale-readiness-gates.md` — all **go** on repeatability + economics.

---

## Stage 4: Scale

| Track | Go criteria | No-go |
|---|---|---|
| **Product** | NRR >110% (enterprise path) or >100% (SMB); release cadence predictable | Churn rising 2+ quarters while hiring |
| **GTM** | Magic number >0.75; AE quota attainment >60% of team | CAC payback >18 mo while scaling S&M |
| **Team** | Correct hire sequence (`sales-team-building`); VP Sales only $2M+ ARR | SDR first hire; VP before repeatability |
| **Capital** | Burn multiple <2x (VC) or profitable (bootstrap); spend per `gtm-spend-management` | Blitz spend without capacity |

**Gate to optimize:** Growth decelerating but economics strong; or $10M+ ARR with Rule of 40 path.

---

## Stage 5: Optimize

| Track | Go criteria | No-go |
|---|---|---|
| **Product** | GRR >90%; expansion playbook documented | Services revenue creeping >15% |
| **GTM** | CAC payback <12 mo; channel mix diversified | Single channel >70% new ARR |
| **Team** | Founder <30% new ARR; succession or VP layer | Key-person risk on enterprise closes |
| **Capital** | Rule of 40 ≥30% (VC) or 20%+ EBITDA (bootstrap/PE) | Efficiency projects deferred for vanity growth |

**Gate to exit optionality:** `exit-potential-scorecard.md` average ≥3.5 and no dimension at 1.

---

## Stage 6: Exit Optionality

| Track | Go criteria | No-go |
|---|---|---|
| **Product** | Roadmap legible to buyers; IP assigned | Contractor IP gaps |
| **GTM** | Repeatable revenue; concentration <15% top logo | "For sale" signaling |
| **Team** | Business runs without founder on every deal | Acqui-hire-only story at $5M+ ARR |
| **Capital** | Modeled after-tax vs 5-yr hold (`financial-modeling`) | Selling only for headline multiple FOMO |

**Go to process:** 3+ exit triggers from `saas-outcomes` Phase 5 + `buyer-readiness-checklist.md` avg ≥4.0.

---

## Parallel Track Health Check (Monthly)

| Track | Green | Yellow | Red |
|---|---|---|---|
| Product | Retention on plan | One segment underperforming | Churn accelerating |
| GTM | Payback on plan | One channel degrading | Economics broken |
| Team | Right hire at right time | Role open >90d critical | Wrong senior hire |
| Capital | Runway >12 mo or profitable | 6–12 mo runway | <6 mo without plan |

**Rule:** Any **red** on primary stage track → do not advance stage. Fix or pivot.
