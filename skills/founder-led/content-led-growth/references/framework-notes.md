## Authoritative foundations

Content-led growth treats content as a **compounding asset** — distribution
(80%) matters more than drafting (20%). One core asset per month fans out to
social, newsletter, podcast, and sales enablement atoms.

## Process phases

- **Phase 1 — Strategy:** ICP × channel × content pillar map
- **Phase 2 — Core asset:** Monthly definitive piece (research, framework, guide)
- **Phase 3 — Derivatives:** 2–3× weekly extracts; daily social atoms
- **Phase 4 — Measure:** Pipeline influence + assisted conversions (not pageviews alone)
