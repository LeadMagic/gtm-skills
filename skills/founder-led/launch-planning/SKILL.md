# Launch Planning

## Overview
A launch is not a press release — it is a coordinated campaign across every channel where your ICP spends attention. This skill plans tiered launches with clear timelines, channel strategies, and success metrics.

## When to Use
- "Plan a product launch"
- "Launch our new feature"
- "Build a pre-launch checklist"
- "Set up Product Hunt launch"
- "Coordinate a multi-channel launch"

## Step-by-Step Process
### Phase 1: Launch Tier Selection
Tier 1 (Major): new product, pivot, rebrand — all channels, press outreach, event.
Tier 2 (Medium): major feature, integration — blog, email, social, in-app.
Tier 3 (Minor): feature update — changelog, in-app notification, email to affected users.

### Phase 2: Pre-Launch (-4 weeks)
Positioning finalized, landing page created, demo video produced, press kit assembled, sales enablement created, customer testimonials gathered.

### Phase 3: Launch Week Execution
Blog post, email to subscribers, social campaign (all channels), press outreach, Product Hunt (if relevant), community posts (Reddit, HN, Twitter, LinkedIn), partner co-marketing.

### Phase 4: Post-Launch (+2 weeks)
Follow-up content, retargeting ads, win/loss tracking on new deals, metrics review, messaging iteration based on response.

## Output Format
Launch plan with timeline, channel strategy, asset checklist, and success metrics.

## Common Pitfalls
1. **Launch without pre-launch** — 4 weeks of prep beats a rushed announcement.
2. **One channel only** — Product Hunt without email + social misses most of your audience.
3. **No follow-up** — launches that end on launch day leave pipeline on the table.
