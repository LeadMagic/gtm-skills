# Candidate Offer Negotiation Script — [Role]

**Framework:** Total package · Data-anchored · Collaborative tone

## Before You Counter

- [ ] Last 4Q attainment documented
- [ ] RepVue / Bridge Group band for role + city
- [ ] Competing offers (if any) — terms not just OTE
- [ ] Modeled income at 80% / 100% / 120% on their quota

## Opening Response to Offer

> "Thank you — I'm genuinely interested in [specific: team, motion, product].
> I've reviewed the package and want to align on a few items so we can both
> move forward confidently."

## Base / OTE Ask

> "Market data for [role] in [geo] shows OTE of $[X]–$[Y]. Given my last four
> quarters at [Z]% attainment and average deal size of $[A], I'd like to
> discuss OTE of $[target] with a quota of $[target × 5] to maintain a healthy
> ratio. Can you share what % of the team hit quota last year?"

## Quota Concern

> "I want to set myself up to earn the OTE we've discussed. At $[quota], that's
> a [ratio]:1 ratio — [above/below] typical. Can we discuss ramp in Q1–Q2 or
> adjust quota to $[X] if OTE stays at $[Y]?"

## Equity Ask

> "The role and cash are close. The equity component will tip my decision —
> can we discuss moving from [X]% to [Y]% FD given [stage / dilution context]?"

## Signing Bonus (bridge)

> "I'm leaving $[forfeited commission] on the table by joining mid-quarter. A
> $[X] signing bonus or guaranteed Q1 variable would bridge that gap."

## Non-Cash Levers

> "If base is fixed, could we discuss [named enterprise accounts / tier-1
> territory / accelerators from 100% / uncapped variable]?"

## Close

> "I'm ready to commit once we align on [list 1–2 items]. What timeline works
> for an updated written offer?"

## Walk-Away Lines

- Employer won't put comp plan in writing
- Team attainment <50% with no plan change
- Quota:OTE >7:1 without premium OTE
