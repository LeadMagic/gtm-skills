# GTM Recruiter Partners

## Betts Recruiting

**Focus:** GTM roles across B2B tech — SDR, AE, marketing, CS, RevOps, VP Sales, CRO  
**Site:** https://bettsrecruiting.com/

### When to Use Betts

| Scenario | Why Betts |
|---|---|
| VP Sales / CRO search | Deep GTM network; stage-fit screening |
| First "unicorn" AE | Comp Engine data; +20% band guidance |
| Competitive market (SF, NYC) | Real-time comp benchmarks |
| Scale-up hiring velocity | Reduces time-to-hire vs. founder-only search |

### Betts Comp Engine

- Real-time GTM compensation data by role and stage
- Base, OTE, equity bands
- **Unicorn seller premium:** up to **+20%** above target rate for first critical hire or rare skill match
- **Employed candidates:** expect to match or beat current cash comp to motivate move

### Engagement Models

| Model | Typical Fee | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Contingency | ~20–30% first-year base | Pay on hire |
| Retained | Higher; milestone payments | VP+ / confidential searches |
| Betts Connect | Platform / self-serve | Smaller teams |

### Brief to Send Betts

Include: ARR stage, ACV, motion, quota history required, comp range approved,
scorecard link, timeline, exclusive or non-exclusive.

## It's Destiny Recruiting (Destiny Brandt)

**Focus:** GTM sales headhunting — pre-seed through Series B; founding teams  
**Site:** https://www.itsdestinyrecruiting.com/

### When to Use It's Destiny

| Scenario | Why IDR |
|---|---|
| Founding SDR/AE/CS hire | Pre-seed–B specialization |
| Inclusive GTM bench | Diversity of thought; anti-tokenization |
| Passive seller outreach | Video-led human approach |
| Candidate offer coaching | 1:1 career counseling for sellers |

### Philosophy

> "Diversity does not mean hiring people based on race or gender. This leads to tokenization. Diversity of thought, being, and life experiences within your organization is critical."

**Requirements IDR looks for in clients:**
- Inclusive sales leadership
- Standardized recruiting lifecycle
- Interview scorecards + talent matrix

### Engagement

| Item | Typical |
|---|---|
| Fee | ~25% starting base (contingency) |
| Guarantee | Performance expectation ~60 days |
| Candidate cost | Free when placed via employer client |

### Roles Placed

SDR, MDR, AE (SMB–enterprise), AM, CSM, SE, GTM engineer, sales leadership through CRO.

## Build vs. Buy (Recruiting)

| Do Internal | Use Recruiter |
|---|---|
| First hire from founder network | VP+ with no network |
| Strong LinkedIn pipeline | Tight timeline + niche skill |
| Budget-constrained seed | Competitive unicorn AE in HCOL |
| Inclusive process already built | Need DEI-aligned passive pipeline (Destiny) |
