# Inclusive GTM Recruiting

Primary partner framework: **It's Destiny Recruiting (Destiny Brandt)**  
Aligned with: Google re:Work, Mark Roberge structured hiring, Kim Scott feedback culture.

## Core Principle

**Diversity of thought** — varied life experiences, backgrounds, and perspectives
on the same performance bar. **Not tokenization** — hiring for demographic
checkbox without culture, scorecards, or support.

> Source: https://www.itsdestinyrecruiting.com/ — "Diversity does not mean hiring people based on race or gender. This leads to tokenization."

## What Inclusive GTM Recruiting Looks Like

| Practice | Anti-pattern |
|---|---|
| Same interview questions for all | Different bar per candidate |
| Scorecard before debrief | "Culture fit" gut feel |
| Work sample (mock discovery) | "Would I get a beer with them?" |
| Published comp ranges | Negotiate from zero — widens gap |
| Diverse interview panel | Homogeneous team decides alone |
| Talent matrix by funnel stage | Only measure headcount at hire |
| Inclusive leadership required | Toxic VP + diverse IC hires |

## Structured Process (re:Work + Destiny)

1. **Role scorecard** — competencies weighted before seeing candidates
2. **Work sample** — mock call, pipeline review, or case (`hiring-by-role`)
3. **Independent scoring** — interviewers score before group discussion
4. **Backchannel references** — especially for leaders; ask about inclusivity
5. **Talent matrix** — track pass rates by stage; fix funnel leaks, not standards

## JD Inclusivity (`job-posting-strategy`)

- Outcomes over years ("closed $500K ARR" not "5+ years")
- No gendered language (Textio audit optional)
- Transparent comp range — required in many states; reduces pay gap at offer
- No "rockstar/ninja" — repels diverse experienced candidates

## Partner Alignment (It's Destiny)

IDR partners with employers who offer:
- Inclusive sales leadership
- Standardized recruiting lifecycle
- Interview scorecards

If leadership won't run structured inclusive process, fix management layer first
(`gtm-leadership`) before diversity sourcing.

## Metrics That Matter

| Metric | Use |
|---|---|
| Pipeline diversity by stage | Where candidates drop |
| Offer accept rate by source | Comp/process issues |
| 12-month attainment by cohort | Bar was consistent, not lowered |
| Voluntary attrition Year 1 | Culture/inclusivity signal |

**Don't:** Set demographic hire quotas without process — drives tokenization.

## Roberge Traits Apply to Everyone

Coachable, Curious, Prior success, Intelligent, Work ethic — score with
evidence, not pedigree. "Prior success" = attainment %, not university brand.
