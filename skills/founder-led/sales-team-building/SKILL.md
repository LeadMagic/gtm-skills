# Sales Team Building

## Overview
Sales hiring out of order is the most expensive mistake in SaaS. VP Sales before $2M ARR has a 70% failure rate. SDR as first hire means the founder manages a junior rep while still closing every deal. This skill provides the correct hiring sequence, POD design, and compensation models drawn from Winning by Design, SaaStr, and QuotaSignal data.

## When to Use
- "When should I hire my first salesperson?"
- "Build my sales team structure"
- "Design AE compensation"
- "Set up sales PODs"
- "Scale from founder-led to team"

## Step-by-Step Process
### Phase 1: WbD GTM Index Self-Assessment
Score your GTM maturity 1-10 across 6 models: Revenue, Data, Math, Operating, Growth, GTM. Score below 6: fix fundamentals before scaling the team.

### Phase 2: Hiring Sequence by ARR
- **Founder closes 10-20 deals** → validated motion exists
- **<$500K ARR**: Senior AE (full-stack, 3-6 yrs, SDR background preferred, $120-160K OTE)
- **$500K-2M ARR**: Second AE → then SDRs to feed pipeline
- **$2M+ ARR, 2+ reps at quota**: Player-coach Head of Sales
- **$5M+ ARR, 8+ reps**: VP Sales, Sales Ops, Enablement

### Phase 3: POD Design (WbD)
SDR:AE:CSM ratios by product complexity. Simple product: 2 SDRs : 3 AEs : 1 CSM. Complex enterprise: 1 SDR : 1 AE : 1 CSM. Ramp time: 3-4 months to 80% productivity.

### Phase 4: Compensation Models
- 50/50 base/variable standard
- AE OTE ≤ 20% of year-1 ARR quota (OTE $120K → quota $600K)
- Linear, accelerated, or split models
- Draw for first 90 days during ramp

### Phase 5: REKS Coaching
Results → Efforts → Knowledge → Skills. Diagnose performance gaps before coaching. Study top performers to identify leverage points.

## Output Format
Sales team building plan with hiring sequence, org chart, compensation model, ramp plan (30/60/90 day), and coaching cadence.

## Common Pitfalls
1. **VP Sales before $2M ARR** — 70% failure rate. Cost: $300-500K in comp + opportunity cost.
2. **SDR as first hire** — founder closes every deal + manages junior rep = neither done well.
3. **No written sales process** — the first AE builds the playbook, not just closes.
4. **AE mis-hire** — 40% mis-hire rate, $484K cost over 24 months (QuotaSignal data).
