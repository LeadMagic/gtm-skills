# Candidate Questions to Ask — Both Sides

*For candidates evaluating offers AND interviewers judging candidate quality. Strong candidates ask hard questions.*

**Role:** ___ **Company stage:** ___ **Interview stage:** Screen / Panel / Final

---

## Why this matters (interviewer lens)

| Candidate asks… | Signal |
|---|---|
| Quota attainment % for team | Serious about earnability |
| Ramp time + certification | Plans for success |
| Why last person left | Confident, direct |
| Nothing | Low research or desperate |

**Betts / Roberge:** Candidates who don't ask about quota math often fail attainment — not because they're weak, but because comp was mis-set.

---

## Stage 1 — Recruiter / screen

### Candidate should ask

1. What is the published OTE range and quota for this role?
2. What % of team hit quota last 4 quarters?
3. What is the sales motion — inbound, outbound, PLG assist?
4. Why is the role open?
5. What are next interview steps and timeline?

### Interviewer should answer honestly

- Publish range — hidden comp kills close rate (`gtm-recruiting`)
- If attainment <50%, say so + what fix is in flight
- Timeline: 48–72h post-final if possible

---

## Stage 2 — Hiring manager

### Candidate should ask

6. What does week 1 and the 30-60-90 look like?
7. How do you coach — frequency, format, tools?
8. Territory / list source — who owns enrichment and lists?
9. What's in the CRM stack? Hygiene expectations?
10. How is marketing generating pipeline for this seat?
11. Path to promotion — AE → senior → manager?
12. What separates top performer from median on your team?

### Red-flag answers (candidate walk away)

| Answer | Problem |
|---|---|
| "Quota is confidential until hire" | Opacity |
| "Nobody really hits but top reps do great" | Broken plan |
| "We don't have onboarding" | Ramp roulette |
| "CRM is optional" | Forecast chaos |

---

## Stage 3 — Panel / executive

### Candidate should ask

13. Company ARR, growth rate, runway (if private — ranges OK)
14. Biggest GTM priority this year?
15. How do product and sales interact on roadmap?
16. Why did you join? What keeps you here?
17. What would you fix in GTM if you had a magic wand?

### For VP+ candidates also ask

18. Board expectations for next 12 months?
19. RevOps / marketing / CS maturity?
20. What happened to the last person in this role?

---

## Stage 4 — References (candidate asks company)

*Candidates should request:*

- Speak with 2 AEs who report to hiring manager (not only exec refs)
- Customer reference if enterprise AE

*Company should offer:* peer conversation pre-offer when possible.

## Candidate self-check before accepting

- [ ] OTE × expected attainment = acceptable cash
- [ ] Quota verified against Bridge 5:1 rule (`gtm-role-descriptions`)
- [ ] Ramp and certification documented (`revenue-team-onboarding`)
- [ ] Equity: shares, FD%, vesting, 409A/post-money explained
- [ ] Competing offers disclosed — negotiation via `founder-comp-playbook` / `gtm-recruiting`

Negotiation script: `gtm-recruiting` → `candidate-negotiation-script.md`
