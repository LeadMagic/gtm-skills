## Universal screen (all GTM roles — 30 min)

| # | Question | Look for |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Walk me through your last 4 quarters — quota/goal, attainment %, why up or down | Specific numbers; ownership not "we" |
| 2 | Tell me about the deal or outcome you're most proud of. Your role? | Individual contribution |
| 3 | Tell me about one you lost. What happened? What did you learn? | Accountability, no blame spiral |
| 4 | Why this company, this role, now? | Research depth; motivation fit |
| 5 | What questions do you have for me? | Quality of questions (`candidate-questions-to-ask.md`) |

**Roberge traits probe (pick 2):**
- Coachability: "Best feedback you received last year — what changed?"
- Curiosity: "What have you learned about our space in the last week?"
- Work ethic: "What does a high-output week look like for you?"

## AE — additional stages

### Numbers deep dive (15 min)

9. "Average deal size, cycle length, win rate last 4 quarters?"
10. "How much pipeline was self-sourced vs marketing?"

### Mock discovery (45 min — panel role-plays prospect)

11. Run discovery — **do not pitch**. Panel scores SPICED coverage.

| Dimension | 1 | 5 |
|---|---|---|
| Questions | Feature pitch early | Situation, pain, impact, decision |
| Listening | Talks 80% | Talks <40% |
| Next step | Vague | Mutual action plan |

### Mock demo (45 min)

12. Present [product] to [persona]. Tailor to pain from discovery.

### Commercial

13. "How do you multithread when champion goes dark?"
14. "Tell me about a deal you walked away from."

## GTM Engineer — additional

*Full scorecard + work sample rubric: `gtm-engineer-scorecard.md`*  
*Role definition: `gtm-role-descriptions` → `references/gtm-engineer-hiring.md`*

### Screen + portfolio

29. "Walk me through a GTM workflow you shipped. What metric moved?"
30. "How do you structure enrichment — providers, verify, dedupe, CRM push?"
31. "GTM Engineer vs RevOps vs Sales Engineer — where do you draw the line?"
32. **Portfolio required** — Loom/GitHub/doc of production workflow

### Work sample (paid, 3–4 hrs)

Enrich 50 accounts → ICP score → CRM push + field mapping doc. Score verify step,
error handling, credit cost, documentation.

### Technical + judgment

33. "Why this provider order? What if hit rate is 40%?"
34. "How would this scale to 50K accounts monthly?"
35. "Design a [funding/hiring/job-change] signal play — trigger to sequence in 5 steps."
36. "Marketing wants more volume; Sales says lists are junk — week 1 plan?"

### Tool signals

| Tool | Strong | Weak |
|---|---|---|
| Clay | Loops, credit model, Claygent | Manual one-off lookups |
| n8n | Error workflows, retries | Never used |
| CRM | Validation rules, stage gates | Export-only |
