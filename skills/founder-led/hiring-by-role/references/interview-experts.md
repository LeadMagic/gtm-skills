## Structured interview rules (re:Work)

1. **Same questions** for every candidate in a role
2. **Score independently** before panel discussion
3. **Work sample** required — mock discovery, case, or async task
4. **3+ interviewers** — at least one outside hiring team
5. **No illegal questions** — age, family, religion, health, national origin

---

## Illegal / risky questions (US — consult counsel)

| Never ask | Ask instead |
|---|---|
| Marital status, kids, pregnancy plans | "Can you travel X% / work these hours?" |
| Age, graduation year (proxy) | Role-relevant experience only |
| Religion, national origin | Work authorization only (HR) |
| Disability, medical history | "Can you perform essential functions with accommodation?" (HR) |
| Past salary (banned many states) | "Our range for this role is $X–$Y OTE" |

`employment-compliance` for offer and classification.
