# GTM Comp Benchmarks — H1 2026 Snapshot

**Sources:** Bridge Group SaaS Sales Metrics (2024–2025 surveys), Betts Comp Engine, RepVue/Compgauge crowdsourced data, Pavilion CRO Council (directional), OpenView / SaaStr market commentary.

**How to use:** Directional US B2B SaaS bands. Validate at offer time with Comp Engine or RepVue for your geo and ACV. Adjust ±10% for SF/NYC.
