# Founder Negotiation Scripts

**Tone:** Direct, data-backed, respectful. Founders sell the **trajectory** when cash lags market.

## Opening (Verbal Yes → Written Offer)

> "We're excited to move forward. I'm sending a written package today with base,
> OTE, quota at 5:1, equity grant with FD%, and Q1–Q2 ramp. I want you to
> model earnings at 80% and 100% attainment — let's review together Thursday."

## Counter: Higher OTE

> "I hear you on $160K vs our $145K OTE. Our comp philosophy is quota:OTE at
> 5:1 — so $160K pairs with $800K quota. Your background supports that. If
> $145K is the right base for year one, we can add $10K signing and 0.05% more
> equity with a refresh review at 12 months if you're at quota."

## Counter: Match Competing Offer

> "Help me understand their plan — base, variable, quota, equity. If it's apples
> to apples on **earnability**, I'll take it to our board tonight. If they're
> offering higher OTE with 2× quota, that's not the same package — let's model both."

## Equity Explanation (Honest)

> "You're getting 45,000 options at $0.42 strike. That's ~0.55% fully diluted
> today. Last 409A post was $8M — paper value is directional, not guaranteed.
> Vesting is 4 years, 1-year cliff. We do refresh grants for performers at year 2."

## Cash-Tight Bootstrap

> "We're profitable at $1.2M ARR and keeping burn low. Cash is $145K OTE with
> aggressive accelerators above 100% — our top rep earned $158K last year at
> 112% attainment. Equity is meaningful: 0.6% FD with pro-rata on future rounds."

## VP / Executive

> "This is a VP Sales scope, not CRO — org ARR gate plus hiring milestones in
> variable. Pavilion peers at our stage band OTE $240–280K; we're at $255K with
> 0.75% FD. Severance and clawback are in the exhibit — counsel reviewed."

## Walk Away

> "I don't think we can align on earnable comp without breaking our economics
> for the team. I respect your bar — let's stay in touch if circumstances change."

## What Founders Should Ask Candidates

1. "Last 4 quarters: quota and attainment %?"
2. "What are you earning today — base and variable realized?"
3. "What would make you say yes besides cash?"
4. "What's your deadline on the other offer?"

Non-negotiable for sales: attainment history.
