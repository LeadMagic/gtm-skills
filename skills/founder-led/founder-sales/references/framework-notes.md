## Authoritative foundations

### Jason Lemkin & Mark Roberge — From Survival to Thrival (Survival Phase)
*Survival to Thrival* defines Survival as $0–$2M ARR where the founder is the
sales team. Lemkin's rule: close 10–20 deals yourself before any sales hire.
Roberge's rule: document every step of those deals — the playbook you write here
becomes the training material for your first AE.

### Mark Roberge — The Sales Acceleration Formula
Treat sales as a science: measure conversion at every stage, hire for
coachability over experience, never delegate until the process is repeatable.

## Process phases

- **Phase 1 — Learn (Proto-Sales):** Discovery conversations; learn what problems people pay for
- **Phase 2 — Repeat (Founder-Led):** Document playbook; optimize problem→value pattern
- **Phase 3 — Systematize:** CRM stages, email templates, objection library from real calls
- **Phase 4 — Prove:** 10–20 founder closes with written process before hiring AE
