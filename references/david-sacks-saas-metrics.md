## Burn Multiple (Canonical)

```
Burn Multiple = Net Burn ÷ Net New ARR
```

| Stage | Target | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| Seed / A | < 2x | Acceptable if growth strong |
| Series B+ | < 1.5x | Efficiency expected |
| PE / late | < 1x | Capital efficiency premium |

**Jason Lemkin popularized** burn multiple framing with Sacks on SaaStr — cite both in board decks.

## Operating Philosophy

- **Default alive vs default dead** — know months of runway at current burn
- **Efficiency over growth-at-all-costs** (2022+ regime) — burn multiple matters more than vanity ARR
- **Pick metrics that drive decisions** — one dashboard for board, not 50 charts

**Contrast Jason Lemkin:** Lemkin = when to hire, founder sales stages. Sacks = capital efficiency math for investors.
