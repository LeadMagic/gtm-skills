## Core Thesis

Outbound breaks when teams optimize for **meetings booked** instead of **completed conversations**. Every real conversation lands in exactly one disposition bucket. Distribution of those buckets diagnoses list, message, rep, or follow-up problems — not gut feel.

> "Stop buying buyer intent. Create your own." — Gilkey (first-party conversation data compounds into market intelligence)

---

## Disposition Science — Six Buckets

Every completed conversation gets one disposition. If it does not fit a bucket, it was not a real conversation.

| Disposition | Meaning | Diagnostic signal |
|---|---|---|
| **Meeting Scheduled** | Confirmed calendar event | Healthy conversion when paired with Activated |
| **Activated** | Interest, intrigue, or intent — not yet booked | Message/rep working; follow-up is the lever |
| **Not Now** | Valid timing objection; future potential | Nurture + re-bucket; not a list failure |
| **Not Me** | Wrong person; no referral obtained | **List/title targeting error** |
| **Referred** | Wrong person but correct contact obtained | **List refinement gold** — build next list from referrals |
| **Not Interested** | Heard pitch; no fit | Segment by rep before blaming message |

**Minimum sample:** 50+ completions before disposition distribution is actionable. Test one variable (list *or* message, not both) per iteration.

---

## King of the Hill (Campaign Optimization)

1. Current campaign = the **king**
2. Read disposition distribution → form hypothesis
3. Change **one** lever (List, Message, Rep, or Follow-Up)
4. Run next iteration; if distribution improves → new king; if worse → revert and test again

### Four Levers (always in play)

1. **List** — right people, right titles, right segmentation depth
2. **Message** — right words, timing, channel
3. **Rep** — execution, prioritization, rapport
4. **Follow-Up** — correct next step when Activated or Not Now

## Phone Intent & The 20% Rule

Only ~**20%** of a B2B market will ever answer a cold call. The other 80% will not pick up regardless of script or rep skill.

- **Phone Intent™ scoring** (TitanX): predict who answers before you dial
- **Precision dialing** over parallel dialing — one contact at a time with full context
- **Channel match:** high phone-intent → phone-heavy; low phone-intent → email/LinkedIn; bad data → nurture, not rep time
- Target **25%+ connect rates** on scored lists vs 3–5% on blind lists

## Conversation-as-KPI Pillars (TitanX Outbound Model)

1. **Conversation as KPI** — track completions, not dials or meetings alone
2. **Disposition Buckets** — every touch categorized (above)
3. **Channel Validation** — test phone, email, LinkedIn combinations for your market
4. **AE Involvement** — AEs stay close to first-party conversation data, not second-hand SDR notes

## Pre-Call Bucket Assignment Checklist

- [ ] Contact scored for Phone Intent (P1 / P2 / P3 / bad data)
- [ ] ICP + persona validated before first dial
- [ ] Direct dial verified (not switchboard)
- [ ] Disposition field ready in CRM before dialing block
- [ ] Referral destinations from prior Referred/Not Me runs captured for list v2

## When Phone Beats Email (and Vice Versa)

| Prefer phone | Prefer email/LinkedIn |
|---|---|
| High Phone Intent contacts | Low/no phone intent |
| Time-sensitive trigger (funding, leadership change) | Technical buyers who research async first |
| Referral follow-up from Referred disposition | Nurture after Not Now |
| Validating message-market fit (Gilkey + Pessar overlap) | Long complex deals needing written context |

Phone and email are **complementary** — disposition data tells you which channel each segment deserves.

---

## Anti-Patterns

1. **Meetings-booked comp** — forces junk meetings; optimize conversations + dispositions instead
2. **Parallel dial without intent scoring** — spam flags, dead air, burned TAM
3. **Changing list and message simultaneously** — cannot attribute disposition shifts
4. **Drawing conclusions below 50 completions** — disposition noise, not signal
5. **Ignoring Referred/Not Me ratios** — highest-signal list-quality diagnostics
