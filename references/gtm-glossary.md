# GTM Glossary

Shared terminology reference for all skills.

## Sales Methodologies

- **MEDDICC** (Force Management) — Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Criteria, Decision Process, Identify Pain, Champion. Enterprise qualification framework. MEDDPICC adds Paper Process and Competition.
- **Command of the Message®** (Force Management) — Value Messaging Framework: Before Scenario, Negative Consequences, After Scenario, Positive Business Outcomes, Required Capabilities, Defensible Differentiators.
- **SPICED** (Winning by Design) — Situation, Pain, Impact, Critical Event, Decision. Full customer lifecycle methodology.
- **SPIN** (Huthwaite/Neil Rackham) — Situation, Problem, Implication, Need-Payoff. Discovery questioning sequence backed by 35,000-call study.
- **Challenger Sale** (Gartner/CEB) — Teach, Tailor, Take Control. Insight-led selling for complex B2B.
- **Sandler** — Up-Front Contracts, Pain Funnel, 7-step Submarine. Mutual qualification system.

## GTM Architecture

- **Bowtie Model** (Winning by Design) — Revenue visualization that extends the funnel through retention and expansion.
- **GTM Index** (Winning by Design) — 1-10 scoring across 6 models: Revenue, Data, Math, Operating, Growth, GTM.
- **POD** (Winning by Design) — Sales unit combining SDRs, AEs, and CSMs with defined ratios.
- **Waterfall Enrichment** — Chaining multiple data providers in sequence, each only firing when the previous returns empty.
- **ICP** — Ideal Customer Profile. Firmographic + technographic + behavioral fit criteria.
- **TAM** — Total Addressable Market.

## SaaS Metrics

- **MRR** — Monthly Recurring Revenue
- **ARR** — Annual Recurring Revenue
- **ARPA** — Average Revenue Per Account
- **NRR** — Net Revenue Retention
- **LTV** — Customer Lifetime Value
- **CAC** — Customer Acquisition Cost
- **ACV** — Annual Contract Value
- **TTFV** — Time To First Value

## Outbound

- **Sequence/Cadence** — Multi-touch outreach schedule across one or more channels
- **SDR/BDR** — Sales/Business Development Representative. Prospecting role.
- **AE** — Account Executive. Closing role.
- **SQL** — Sales Qualified Lead
- **MQL** — Marketing Qualified Lead

## Enrichment & Data

- **Firmographics** — Company attributes: size, industry, revenue, location
- **Technographics** — Technology stack a company uses
- **Intent Data** — Signals indicating purchase readiness
- **Catch-All Domain** — Email server that accepts all incoming mail regardless of mailbox existence
- **Bounce Rate** — Percentage of sent emails that fail to deliver

## Design & Asset Terminology

- **Battlecard** — Concise competitive comparison document for sales teams
- **One-Pager** — Single-page product/solution overview for prospects
- **FIA Framework** (Klue) — Fact → Impact → Act. Battlecard structure.
- **Leave-Behind** — Post-meeting document that reinforces value and next steps
- **Pitch Deck** — 10-15 slide presentation for discovery and demo calls
