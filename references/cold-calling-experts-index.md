## Recommended Stacks

**Cold call program (greenfield):**
Gilkey Phone Intent list → Reisert Buckets CRM setup → Pessar script/tonality → Slocum 3×3 prep + call blocks → Blount Golden Hours calendar policy

**Fix low connect rates:**
Gilkey Phone Intent + list lever → disposition distribution after 50 completions

**SDR team ramp:**
Pessar ColdCall-Market Fit pilot → Reisert Buckets → Slocum coaching → `revenue-team-onboarding` certification

**Phone + email (complementary):**
`cold-calling` (this index) + `cold-email-strategy` (Crawford/Holland/Guillaume) + `multi-channel-outreach`

## Phone vs Email Quick Router

| Signal | Lead with |
|---|---|
| High Phone Intent (Gilkey P1) | Phone (Reisert Bucket 2→3 cadence) |
| Low phone intent | Email/LI (Guillaume multichannel) |
| Not Me / Referred dispositions | List rebuild (Gilkey) before more dials |
| Message-market fit unknown | Pessar Call Pilot before hiring |
| Rep skill gap | Slocum intensive + CallBlitz live coaching |
