## 2026 Free-to-Paid Conversion Report (Key Uses)

- Benchmark **your** free→paid rate vs segment — not vanity signup counts
- Separate **freemium** vs **free trial** vs **reverse trial** models
- Tie conversion to **activation event** quality (Verna PQL alignment)
- Full-funnel math: signup → activation → paid → expansion

**Do not** optimize conversion rate in isolation — pair with CAC payback (`David Skok`).
