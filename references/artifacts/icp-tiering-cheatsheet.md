# ICP Tiering Cheatsheet

| Tier | Typical Company Size | Buying Motion | Message Style | Proof Needed | Risk |
|---|---:|---|---|---|---|
| Small Business | 1-50 employees | Owner-led, fast decision | Concrete pain, fast setup, price clarity | Reviews, screenshots, simple ROI | Low budget, high churn |
| Mid-Market | 50-500 employees | Department buyer + champion | Workflow fit, team productivity, integration | Case studies, security docs, business case | Multi-stakeholder drift |
| Enterprise | 500+ employees | Committee + procurement | Risk reduction, standardization, governance | Reference customers, compliance artifacts, executive narrative | Slow cycle, consensus loss |

Use tiering to adapt every recommendation: ICP, offer, sales process, onboarding, success motion, and metrics.
