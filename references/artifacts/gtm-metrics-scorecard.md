# GTM Metrics Scorecard

Use this artifact to standardize GTM metrics across skills.

| Metric | Formula | Applies To | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pipeline Created | Qualified opportunities created in period | Sales-led, hybrid | Segment by source and ICP tier |
| Win Rate | Closed won / qualified opportunities | All motions | Report by ACV band and segment |
| Sales Cycle | Median days from qualified opportunity to closed won | Sales-led | Use median, not only average |
| CAC Payback | CAC / gross profit from new ARR or revenue | SaaS | Finance-owned; keep assumptions explicit |
| Activation Rate | Accounts reaching first meaningful outcome / signups | PLG | Define activation event before measuring |
| Expansion Rate | Expansion revenue / existing customer base | CS, growth | Separate seat, usage, and product expansion |
| Retention | Logo and revenue retention | CS, lifecycle | Include cohort window |

Never present metrics as accounting, tax, legal, or financial advice. Treat formulas as operating guides and ask the user for their actual definitions when precision matters.
