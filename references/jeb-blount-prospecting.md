## Core Model — The Law of Replacement

Every day you must **replace** pipeline that dies. Prospecting is not optional overhead — it is the **#1 failure point** for sales careers.

| Principle | Rule | Ops implication |
|---|---|---|
| **Law of Replacement** | Add new opportunities daily or pipeline shrinks | CRM "new conversations" KPI |
| **Golden Hours** | Block 2–3 hours/day for prospecting before reactive work | Calendar policy for SDR/AE |
| **Omni-channel** | Phone + email + social — not single-channel hope | Align with Reisert buckets + Guillaume multichannel |
| **One-call close myth** | Most deals need persistent, professional follow-up | Disposition Science over vanity dials |

## Five Prospecting Channels (Blount)

Rank effort by **where your ICP responds**, not rep preference:

1. **Telephone** — highest real-time conversion when Phone Intent is high (pair Gilkey)
2. **Email** — scale + async; pair Crawford/Holland message quality
3. **Text/SMS** — where compliant and ICP-appropriate
4. **Social (LinkedIn)** — warm-up and credibility, not spam DMs
5. **In-person** — field/enterprise only

**Phone stack in repo:** Gilkey Phone Intent → Reisert CRM Buckets → Pessar script → Slocum 3×3 → **Blount Golden Hours discipline**

## Fanatical Prospecting Daily Cadence

| Block | Duration | Activity |
|---|---|---|
| Golden Hours (AM) | 90–120 min | New outreach — phone + email per bucket priority |
| Midday | 30 min | Social touches, referrals from dispositions |
| Golden Hours (PM) | 60–90 min | Follow-up cadence, voicemail + email pairs |
| Admin | End of day | CRM hygiene, next-day list prep |

**Metric:** Meaningful conversations + new opportunities created — not raw dial count alone.

## When to Use

- SDR/AE not filling top of funnel
- Reps "too busy" to prospect — enforce Golden Hours
- Building phone-first culture alongside `cold-calling` experts
- AE pipeline generation (not SDR-only)

## When NOT to Use

- Replacing **Gilkey Disposition Science** diagnosis — Blount is discipline; Gilkey is list/message fit
- Pure PLG inbound-only motions at early scale
