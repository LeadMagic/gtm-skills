## Core Philosophy — B2B Marketing That Feels Human

| Principle | Practice | Anti-pattern |
|---|---|---|
| **No jargon** | Write like you talk | "Synergize enterprise solutions" |
| **Founder/brand voice** | Real people behind the brand | Stock photo corporate |
| **Community over campaigns** | Exit Five = peer learning | One-off webinar lead gen |
| **Brand before demand** | Long-term trust | CPL-only LinkedIn ads |

**Contrast Chris Walker:** Walker = dark social / demand creation measurement. Gerhardt = **brand voice + community** for B2B marketers. Complementary — Pattern 26 + content-led growth.

## Exit Five Content Patterns

1. **Opinionated POV** — take a stand on how marketing should work
2. **Practitioner stories** — CMO guests, not vendor pitches
3. **Repurpose audio → written** — podcast clips to LinkedIn posts
4. **Member-led learning** — community as product

Apply to `content-led-growth`, `founder-brand`, `vibe-marketing`.
