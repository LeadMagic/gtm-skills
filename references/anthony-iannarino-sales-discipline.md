## Core Model — Discipline Over Hacks

Sales success = **mindset + skill set + tool set** — in that order. No tactic fixes weak fundamentals.

### The Only Sales Guide — Four Foundations

| Pillar | Behaviors |
|---|---|
| **Self-discipline** | Show up, follow process, no excuse culture |
| **Accountability** | Own outcomes; pipeline honesty |
| **Optimism** | Resilience through losses |
| **Caring** | Genuine interest in customer outcomes |

---

## Eat Their Lunch — Leveling Up Value

When competing against incumbents:

1. **Level 1:** Match their offering
2. **Level 2:** Differentiate features (weak)
3. **Level 3:** **Level up** — teach buyer why their current approach costs them
4. **Level 4:** Strategic partner — co-own business outcome

Maps to **Challenger teaching** (Adamson) — Iannarino emphasizes **consistency and call discipline**; Adamson emphasizes **insight content**.
