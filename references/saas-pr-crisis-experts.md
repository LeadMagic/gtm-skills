## When to hire external PR

| Signal | Action |
|---|---|
| Story spreading across tier-1 press + social | Retained crisis counsel (Highwire, Offleash, or equivalent) |
| Confirmed breach with unclear notification scope | PR + legal jointly |
| CEO lacks comms bandwidth | Fractional comms lead or agency |
| Deal pipeline citing incident in late stage | CS + sales FAQ + optional analyst briefing |

**DIY is fine for:** Sev 1–2 outages with status page discipline, single negative reviews, planned exec transitions with clean narrative.
