## Desired Outcome Framework

Customer success is not "make them happy" — it is **delivering the outcome they bought**.

```
Required Outcome = Desired Outcome + Appropriate Experience
```

| Term | Definition | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Desired Outcome** | Measurable business result customer needs | Reduce churn 15% in 90 days |
| **Appropriate Experience** | How they want to get there | Self-serve vs high-touch |
| **Required Outcome** | Both together — your success criteria | Outcome + experience contract |

**Handoff rule:** Sales must document Desired Outcome before CS kickoff — SPICED Impact maps here.

## Success Gap

| Segment | Behavior | CS action |
|---|---|---|
| **Successful customers** | Using product correctly → getting outcome | Expansion, advocacy |
| **Struggling customers** | Using correctly → not getting outcome | Success plan, services |
| **Failure-to-launch** | Not using correctly | Onboarding fix, training |
| **Churn risk** | Wrong fit or abandoned | Save play or qualify out |

Diagnose **which gap** before prescribing playbooks.
