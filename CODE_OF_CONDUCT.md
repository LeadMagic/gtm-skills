# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Keep contributions professional, specific, and useful. This repo exists to ship practical GTM skills for AI agents.

Acceptable behavior:

- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Give concrete examples and reproducible failures.
- Respect privacy and do not post private data.
- Assume public visibility for every comment, issue, and PR.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, astroturfing, or vendor promotion disguised as a skill.
- Deliberately submitting secrets, tracking code, malicious scripts, or prompt-injection content.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may close issues, reject PRs, edit/delete comments, or block contributors who degrade repo quality or safety.
